Brown Wins CAL Player of the Week Honors
Monday, September 3rd , 2035
The California League presented its Player of the Week Award to Fresno Grizzlies third baseman Brad Brown.

"His numbers speak for themselves," a club publicist said. "He's been really seeing the ball well."

Indeed, Brown has. Last week he went 8 for 19, batted .421 with 2 home runs and 4 RBIs.

In 79 games this season, Brown has compiled a .269 batting average with 81 hits and 10 home runs. He has also scored 35 times and knocked in 42 runs.
