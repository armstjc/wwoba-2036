KFL Hands Out Reliever of the Year
Wednesday, October 10th , 2035
He may only be 22, but Dae-hyun Kim of the Ganghwa Landers has already established himself as one of the elite relievers in the Korean Futures League.

The reliever was today named the KFL Reliever of the Year winner for 2035.

He won the award with a strong year that saw him put up 23 saves in 34 relief appearances and 43 strikeouts in 36 innings. He had a record of 2-0 to go with his 1.00 ERA.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Jung-hoon Kim of the Goyang Heroes finished second in voting, while Keun-ho Hong of the Hampyeong Tigers finished third.
