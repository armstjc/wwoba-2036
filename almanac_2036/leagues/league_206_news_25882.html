Spokane's Justin Toole Named Top NWL Manager
Friday, October 3rd , 2036
Spokane manager Justin Toole caused quite a stir when he came to town. Noted for his feisty, no-nonsense attitude, Toole threw his all into the 2036 campaign, winning over critics and fans as he went. He also won a few ballgames, running up a 78-54 record, which was good enough to take the first place spot in the Northwest League standings.

"These guys are grown men. They don't need psychobabble, they need to win ballgames," Toole snapped at his first press conference. While his approach was controversial, even for Spokane, it netted the 50-year-old the Northwest League Manager of the Year trophy. In response to which he replied, "It ain't molecular physics, it's baseball," and began discussing potential free agents with the local media.
