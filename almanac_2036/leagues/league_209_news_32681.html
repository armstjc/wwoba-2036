Lake Elsinore Beats Fresno in Round 2
Tuesday, October 2nd , 2035
This is the time of the baseball season when grown men are reduced to tears. This year, the Lake Elsinore Storm were shedding tears of joy and the Fresno Grizzlies those of sorrow.

The Storm topped the Grizzlies today 5-2 at Chukchansi Park and locked up their 1st California League Round 2 championship, winning the series 4-3.

"To come this far and leave empty-handed is not easy," said Fresno manager Addison Bowman.

The Storm finished the regular season in the Southern Division with a 72-60 record, ending up in first place in the division standings.
