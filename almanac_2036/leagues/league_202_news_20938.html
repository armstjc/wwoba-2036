June's PCL Batter of Month Award Announced
Tuesday, July 1st , 2036
Jim Woods made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Pacific Coast League this past month.

The Albuquerque Isotopes center fielder shined through the month of June and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 23-year-old hit .368 with 6 home runs, 25 RBIs and 14 runs scored.

For the season, Woods is hitting at a .329 clip with 12 home runs, 38 RBIs and has scored 52 runs. He has 75 hits, 58 walks and a .490 on-base percentage.
