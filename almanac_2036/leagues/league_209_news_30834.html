Injury Sidelines Pat Davis for Season
Saturday, August 25th , 2035
The season is ending on a bad note for Pat Davis, starting pitcher for the Modesto Nuts. It was announced today that he will miss the rest of the season because of injury. This season, Davis has worked 93.2 innings with 83 strikeouts en route to a 6-3 record and a 3.65 ERA.
