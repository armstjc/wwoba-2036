Somerset Wins Top Spot in Northeast Division
Tuesday, September 18th , 2035
The pennant has been won, the Somerset Patriots are champions of the Northeast Division.

Right Fielder Jesus Zapata said his team is a "gritty and determined" club that were poised to win the division from day one.

"We knew the day the season opened that we'd be champions," he said. "It was just a matter of going out there and doing it."

At the helm, team manager Andy Barb said his club is certainly not short on confidence.

"There's a lot of belief in the dugout and in the clubhouse," he said.

Somerset's record currently sits at 86-50 and it's the 4th division win for the franchise.
