Marvel Bashes 3 HR for Aberdeen
Sunday, June 22nd , 2036
Tim Marvel was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 14-3 Aberdeen Ironbirds triumph at Daniel S. Frawley Stadium.

The 28-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Ironbirds past the Wilmington Blue Rocks.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Marvel told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The third baseman went 4-for-5, rung up 9 RBIs and scored 4 runs on his record-setting day.

Tim Marvel doubled in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, hit a three-run home run off Changmin Sawyer in the 5th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Sergio Mosquera in the 6th and hit a two-run home run off Sergio Mosquera in the 8th.

This season, Marvel has hit at a .286 clip and produced 26 runs batted in, 6 home runs and 26 runs.
