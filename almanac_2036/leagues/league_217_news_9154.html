Friday, June 1st , 2035
The Pitcher of the Month Award has been announced and the Korean Baseball Organization winner is Lotte hurler, Adam Plutko. For his fine efforts in May he has been named the league's #1 pitcher.

During the month, Plutko excelled with a 1.41 ERA and 4 wins and 1 loss. In 38.1 innings he struck out 27 and gave up 29 hits and 10 walks.

In 10 starts this year, Plutko has fashioned a 5-3 record and a 2.87 ERA.
