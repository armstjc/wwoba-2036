Shuckers Blank Mississippi, Griffin Winner
Monday, August 25th , 2036
The Mississippi Braves manager was philosophical in the clubhouse following a shutout loss to the Biloxi Shuckers. The Braves were overpowered by Biloxi starter Bobby Griffin, who hurled his club to victory, 3-0.

"It was just one of those games -- shake it off and do better next time," Michael Mayer said.

Over 9 innings, Griffin blanked Mississippi on 5 hits with no walks and 6 strikeouts.

"I kept them off balance. I could tell a lot of them were late on fastballs because they were fouling them off. Once you see that, it sets them up for other pitches," Griffin said.

"I set them up, where if they chased one breaking ball, they were going to chase another one."

So far this season, Griffin has a record of 9-2 with 89 strikeouts and 48 walks in 104.2 innings and a 3.70 ERA.
