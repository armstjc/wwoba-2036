In a dominant outing, Ramiro Orantes of the Hartford Yard Goats totally silenced the bats of the Binghamton Rumble Ponies, finishing with a no-hitter.

In the 3-0 victory, Orantes faced only 29 batters, surrendered no hits, had 3 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"Honestly, I didn't even know the situation was what it was until the seventh inning," Hartford manager Will Venable said. "I happened to look up there to see how many hits we had gotten, and I happened to notice that they hadn't gotten any."

"The Yard Goats had all the hits and all the runs, so they won," he snapped. "But yeah, Orantes pitched a heck of a ballgame."

To date this season, Orantes is 6-2 with 16 starts and an ERA of 3.45 with 54 strikeouts in 88.2 innings.
