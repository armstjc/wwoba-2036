Renegades Herebia Named SAL's Best Pitcher
Monday, September 1st , 2036
Upon hearing the news that he had won the South Atlantic League Pitcher of the Month honors for August's work, Hudson Valley reliever Edgar Herebia merely smiled.

"That's just the kind of guy he is, and that's what makes him so good out there," his manager told reporters. "The guy has ice water in his veins. Nothing short of a League Championship could get this guy excited and I'm not so sure that'd do it either."

Herebia made no relief appearances burning through 34 innings as he racked up no saves, 23 strikeouts and held batters to a .185 average as he notched a 4-0 record and a 1.59 ERA.

For the year, Herebia has no relief appearances and a 8-12 record with no saves. He has logged 140.1 innings and allowed 159 base hits. Opposing clubs have put up a .287 batting average against him. Herebia has rung up 113 strikeouts, surrendered 76 walks and fashioned a 5.19 ERA.
