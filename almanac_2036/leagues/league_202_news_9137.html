At only 22 years of age, Alfredo Donis could very well have many more months like this last one during his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Salt Lake Bees, as May was one to remember for the first baseman and he has captured the Pacific Coast League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .407 with 7 home runs, 36 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

So far on the season, Donis is hitting .368 with 9 home runs, 53 RBIs and 37 runs scored. He has 70 hits and played in 49 games.
