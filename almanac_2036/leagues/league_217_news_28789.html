2000 Hits for Park
Wednesday, July 18th , 2035
Seong-han Park started out as a baby-faced rookie many seasons ago. Yesterday at Munhak Baseball Stadium the SSG veteran joined the league's 2000-hit club. Park achieved that milestone with a 2-for-3 day in the Landers 6-1 win over the Kiwoom Heroes.

He spoke to reporters in the clubhouse after the game and said, "Looking at the list of all the great players who have done this is humbling. It is a great honor to be among them."

In 2018 career games Park is hitting at a .269 pace with 2001 hits with 315 doubles, 28 triples and 49 homers while scoring 891 runs and racking up 650 RBIs. He logged 837 walks and owns a .347 on-base percentage.
