West Michigan Hexed: No-Hit by Sobieski
Monday, July 16th , 2035
It was the game that pitchers dream about: the no-hitter.

It happened at Parkview Field. Steve Sobieski of the Fort Wayne TinCaps was right on target against the West Michigan Whitecaps in a 3-0 victory. Sobieski zeroed in on them, holding them hitless with 10 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"I just had it all today," Sobieski told BNN reporters after the game. "Very good stuff and great location. That is a combination that's hard to beat."

This season, Sobieski has worked 32.2 innings with 31 strikeouts en route to a 2-2 record and a 2.20 ERA.
