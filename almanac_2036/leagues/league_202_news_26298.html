PCL Hands Out Reliever of the Year
Tuesday, October 14th , 2036
At the young age of only 24, Mauricio Castanon has been named the Pacific Coast League Reliever of the Year winner for 2036.

The future appears bright for the Reno Aces relief ace, who captured the award with 7 saves in 54 relief appearances, 52 strikeouts in 64.1 innings and a record of 8-5 to go with his 3.36 ERA.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 10. Chris Derezinski of the Sugar Land Space Cowboys finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Ernesto Castillo of the Tacoma Rainiers finished third.
