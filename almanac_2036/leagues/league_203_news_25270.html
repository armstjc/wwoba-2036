Reading Captures Northeast Division Crown
Thursday, September 18th , 2036
Fans of the Reading Fightin Phils can breathe a sigh of relief - the Northeast Division is now clinched.

The postseason race came down to the final days of the season, but in the end Reading has outlasted division rivals to claim the top spot, for the 5th time in franchise history.

"We want to bring the 3rd Round 1 to Reading. For our fans, our players, our coaches, our city, that's what it's all about," said an excited David Partida.

The catcher went on to say that no matter who his team is up against, he considers them favorites.

"I like our chances," he said. "We've got a lot of confidence right now."
