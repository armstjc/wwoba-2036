Hinojosa 5-for-5 for Grizzlies
Wednesday, August 20th , 2036
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-5 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for David Hinojosa, it came around today.

The 21-year-old center fielder scattered hits all over Chukchansi Park to pace the 16-9 Fresno Grizzlies victory over the Inland Empire 66ers.

Inland Empire skipper Angelo Ponte told reporters in the press room that "Hinojosa deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Hinojosa hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

David Hinojosa singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Brad Weaver in the 5th and hit an RBI single in the 6th.

So far this year, Hinojosa has 7 home runs and a .248 batting average.
