Saints Heading to Round 2
Wednesday, October 3rd , 2035
The championship aspirations of the St. Paul Saints are still alive and well, while the Buffalo Bisons are left to wait until next year.

St. Paul won the clinching game of the Round 1 5-0 over Buffalo at CHS Field, to win the series 4-1.

"You feel like a little kid again, celebrating something like this," said second baseman Chris Roller, who paced the St. Paul offense and was named series MVP. "We take everything real seriously when we're on the field, but after a series is over you can finally relax a little bit."

Roller claimed the MVP by racking up 6 hits for a .353 average, with a .389 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, 4 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The St. Paul Saints don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Norfolk Tides - Omaha Storm Chasers series. Omaha leads the series 3 games to 2. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
