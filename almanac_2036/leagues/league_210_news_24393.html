Welles Selected CAR's Best Pitcher
Monday, September 1st , 2036
There was little doubt around the Carolina League that Dave Welles would be awarded Pitcher of the Month honors for August. Today, they made it official, naming the 23-year-old Down East Wood Ducks starter the latest honoree.

He thoroughly dominated the hitters who stood in against him, holding them to a .200 batting average while fashioning a 1.30 ERA in 5 starts. His record for the month was 4-1 in 41.2 innings of work.
