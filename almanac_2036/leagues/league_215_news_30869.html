Phillies Win Round 1
Sunday, August 26th , 2035
The Philadelphia (FCL) Phillies took time to celebrate with the Philadelphia (FCL) fans who stuck around after the game, following the team's Round 1 triumph over the Minnesota (FCL) Twins.

Members of the Phillies hung around chatting, high-fiving and taking pictures with the Philadelphia (FCL) fans in attendance at Hammond Stadium, long after the last pitch had been thrown in their 1-0 win to seal the series 4-1.

"We've got great fan support," said shortstop Alfredo Sarmiento. "Whether we're at home or on the road, the fans support us. So we wanted to show them how important that is to us. Some Minnesota (FCL) fans that were there even congratulated us on the win."

For the series, Sarmiento hit .500 with a .524 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, 6 RBI and scored 3 runs.

Things weren't so cordial on the Twins side of things. Their clubhouse was closed to media after the game and all players and coaches quickly dressed and left the stadium after the end to their season.

The Philadelphia (FCL) Phillies will play against the Tampa Bay (FCL) Rays in the next round. Tampa Bay (FCL) won their series against the Houston (FCL) Astros Orange 4 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
