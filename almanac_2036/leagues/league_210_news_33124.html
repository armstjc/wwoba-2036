Carolina League Top Hurler Announced
Thursday, October 18th , 2035
The Carolina League announced today that Hyeon-wook Kim has been chosen the Outstanding Pitcher Award winner for 2035. The Carolina hurler had everything working for him as he cruised to an outstanding 7-3 record.

Kim put up an impressive 1.71 ERA in his award-winning season. He started 18 times, worked 137 innings, yielded 95 hits, walked 42, fanned 133 and held opposing hitters to a .194 average.

He received 10 first place votes out of a possible 12. Mike Penque of the Columbia Fireflies finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Luis Otero of the Delmarva Shorebirds finished third.
