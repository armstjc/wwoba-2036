Grizzlies Advance to Round 2
Saturday, September 22nd , 2035
California League pundits who figured the Round 1 between the Fresno Grizzlies and Modesto Nuts would go the distance were right on the mark.

The two teams battled back and forth in a spirited series, with Fresno coming out on top thanks to a 6-1 win in the deciding game at John Thurman Field, to wrap things up 4-3.

"You get to the last game and anything can happen, so we're overjoyed to come out on top," said Grizzlies second baseman Jimmy Hasty, who was named series MVP.

For the series, Hasty had 12 hits for a .387 average. He had 2 home runs, 7 RBI and scored 6 runs.

The Fresno Grizzlies will play against the Lake Elsinore Storm in the next round. Lake Elsinore won their series against the Inland Empire 66ers 4 games to 2. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
