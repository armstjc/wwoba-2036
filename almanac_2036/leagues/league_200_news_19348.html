Los Angeles Dazed, Underdue Gets No-Hitter
Saturday, May 31st , 2036
Texas is abuzz in the wake of a masterful no-hitter by Jahiem Underdue.

The 24-year-old hurler was dominating against the Los Angeles Dodgers, leading the Rangers to a 3-0 win at Globe Life Field.

"I could tell early on that it was going to be a long day for us," Los Angeles manager Vinny Pennell said afterward. "He made us look real bad. He was that good. And still going strong at the end of the game."

Underdue had 12 strikeouts and gave up 1 walk en route to the no-hitter.

"Yeah, this was a special day for me," Underdue said. "I had my good stuff and good movement. Good location, too. Even when I made a mistake, I got away with it today."

For the season, Underdue has compiled a 3-5 record with a 3.34 ERA.
