Philadelphia (FCL) Starter August's Best Pitcher
Saturday, September 1st , 2035
The Philadelphia (FCL) Phillies owe Juan Casian a lot for his performance this past month. One of the league's most dominant starters in August, Casian tore up the opposition with a fine 3-0 mark on his way to the Florida Complex League Pitcher of the Month award.

"When you're pitching this well," analyzed Casian, "it's a lot easier to come to the park. I don't judge my performance by the stats, I judge it -- the team judges it -- by the score of the game. I could say I'm tossing electric stuff right now and that would go over big. But if I toss that same stuff -- exact same pitch selection and location -- and they bang me around tomorrow, I was awful. That's the way baseball is."

It was Casian who did the banging in August as he tossed up 24 strikeouts in 17 innings, holding opponents to a .169 average and posting a 1.06 ERA.

To date this season, Casian is 7-2 with 12 starts and an ERA of 2.95 with 80 strikeouts in 61 innings.
