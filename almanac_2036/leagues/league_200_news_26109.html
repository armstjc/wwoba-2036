Cardinals Cruise to Division Series with Sweep
Wednesday, October 8th , 2036
Ezequiel Moreno said he expected the Wild Card Game to be "a battle from start to finish."

In the end, it was nothing more than a minor skirmish, as the St. Louis Cardinals encountered little resistance in sweeping the Chicago Cubs, to advance to the Division Series.

"It may have been a sweep, but it still was a tough series," said Moreno.

St. Louis won the final game by a 16-3 count and were led in the series by Moreno, who was named MVP. On the series, he hit .444 with 1 home runs. He also drove in 6 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The St. Louis Cardinals will play against the Miami Marlins in the next round. Miami had a bye in the Wild Card Game. The Division Series is scheduled to begin on October 10th , 2036.
