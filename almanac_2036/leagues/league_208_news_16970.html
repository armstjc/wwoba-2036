Ortega Cracks 3 HR for Cubs
Sunday, April 20th , 2036
South Bend third baseman Juan Ortega led the Cubs to a triumph, 9-2, over the West Michigan Whitecaps, crushing three home runs.

"He's scary good when he's swinging the bat like that," said South Bend skipper Bryan Paukovits.

Indeed, Ortega put up some "scary good" numbers: three homers, 4 hits in 5 at-bats with 4 runs and 5 RBIs.

"I'm just thinkin' about winning ballgames," Ortega said. "When you think about home runs, you very seldom get 'em."
