Ontiveros Batters Winston-Salem Big Time
Sunday, May 13th , 2035
No matter what the Winston-Salem Dash threw at him, Sergio Ontiveros hit it. He easily handled Dash pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Crawdads triumph, 12-3, at L.P. Frans Stadium.

"The way I was swinging, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Ontiveros's quip, Dash skipper Adam Hackstedt said he "would take him up on that next time."

Sergio Ontiveros hit a two-run home run off Alfredo Rivera in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and doubled in the 8th.

In 27 games this season, Ontiveros has batted at a .225 pace with 25 hits and 6 home runs.
