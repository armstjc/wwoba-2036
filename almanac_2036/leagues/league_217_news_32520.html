Saturday, September 29th , 2035
Say what you want about the Hanwha Eagles and their abilities on the baseball diamond. Nobody can question their ability to celebrate a division title.

The music was loud and the adult beverages were flowing tonight as the Eagles officially clinched the Korean Baseball Organization.

"You concentrate so hard on winning the division, so it's nice to finally be here and be able to enjoy it," said shortstop Hye-min Kim, whose championship t-shirt was drenched. "It's probably going to be a long night."

Hanwha clinched the division with a record currently sitting at 79-60. It is the 2nd division championship in team history and will give the Eagles the opportunity to go for their 3rd Korean Baseball Organization championship.
