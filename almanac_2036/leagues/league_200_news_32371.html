Washington-Los Angeles Brawl, Bly and Smith Suspended
Monday, September 24th , 2035
Josh Bly of the Washington Nationals and Danny Smith of the Los Angeles Dodgers will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 3 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Nationals-Dodgers game at Nationals Park.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Bly and Smith will begin serving them soon.

This year, Bly has socked 6 hits in 62 at-bats for a .097 average with no home runs. He is getting on base at a .111 pace and his totals also include 1 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

On the season, Smith has 2 wins and 3 losses and a 6.23 ERA.
