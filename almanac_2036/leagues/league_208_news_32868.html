Zavodny Wins Award for MWL Dominance
Tuesday, October 9th , 2035
Dave Zavodny said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 23 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the Fort Wayne TinCaps rising star has won the Midwest League Reliever of the Year for 2035.

He captured the award on the strength of his 2.33 ERA over 58 innings. He also had 13 saves in 48 relief appearances, 65 strikeouts and only 38 walks.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 12. Frank Sabala of the Beloit Sky Carp finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Jim Jackson of the Quad Cities River Bandits finished third.
