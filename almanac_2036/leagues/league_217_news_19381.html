Kim Wins KBO Batter of the Month
Sunday, June 1st , 2036
Hwan-hee Kim is likely in no rush to flip the calendar over.

The Samsung right fielder had a huge month in May and has been named the Korean Baseball Organization Batter of the Month.

On the month, he posted a .283 batting average, with 9 home runs, 18 RBIs, 22 runs scored and a .336 on-base percentage.

For the season, Kim has produced 50 hits in 181 at-bats for a .276 average with 12 home runs and gotten on base at a .342 clip.
