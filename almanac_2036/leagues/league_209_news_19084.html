Hoare Stops Lake Elsinore
Saturday, May 24th , 2036
Bobby Hoare was in a mood to talk following the Visalia Rawhide contest against the Lake Elsinore Storm, expounding to BNN reporters on his pitching philosophy. He had good reason to be excitable, having tossed a complete game shutout of the Storm, 15-0.

"There are three important elements in pitching success: location, location, and location," Hoare said. "I was hitting my spots time and time again."

The right-hander blanked Lake Elsinore on a 4-hitter, allowing 1 walk and racking up 14 strikeouts.

This season, Hoare has worked 24.1 innings with 27 strikeouts en route to a 3-1 record and a 1.48 ERA.
