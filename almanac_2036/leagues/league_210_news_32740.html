It's Over! Carolina Defeats Charleston
Thursday, October 4th , 2035
It was a hard-fought battle for both teams to make it this far. But as the saying goes, "to the victors go the spoils." For the victorious Carolina Mudcats, that meant the Carolina League Round 2 trophy.

The Mudcats took a 10-1 victory over the Charleston RiverDogs at Five County Stadium, winning their 3rd championship after taking the series 4-2.

"We've got grit," said Mudcats right fielder Jabari Rolack, who was named MVP of the series. "We know how to win when we have to."

The new titleholders completed the regular season with a first-place finish in the Central Division and a 85-47 record.
