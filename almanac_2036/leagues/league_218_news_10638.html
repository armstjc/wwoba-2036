Tigers End Kim Streak
Tuesday, June 26th , 2035
It was the best of both worlds for the Hampyeong Tigers at Daegu Samsung Lions Park, as they defeated the Gyeongsan Lions, 8-7, and also snapped Deuk-jin Kim's 20-game hitting streak. The 19-year-old Gyeongsan center fielder failed to get a hit in 5 at-bats.

"Nothing personal against Kim, we just wanted to win the game," said Tigers skipper Woo-sung Yang. "We did a great job keeping him off-balance and guessing."

When asked by reporters about his streak coming to an end, Kim frowned and said, "I won't lie and say that I'm not disappointed about it, but I'd feel much better if we had won."

Currently Kim is batting .252 with 4 home runs, 16 RBIs and 29 runs scored.
