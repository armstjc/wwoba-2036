Oh Swats #300
Thursday, May 17th , 2035
Do-hwan Oh circled the bases and raised his arm in triumph. He had finally hit his 300th home run. It was a homer for a lot of people: himself, his teammates past and present, his former managers and coaches, and especially his family, who sat near the plate cheering his every at-bat during the past few games.

Oh spoke with BNN and said, "I knew number 300 would come sooner or later. I had to keep reminding myself, 'Relax, relax, just let it happen, let it happen.' And it finally did."

His milestone homer came in the Colorado Rockies 7-6 win over the Boston Red Sox. Oh went 3 for 4 in the game, scored 2 runs, and batted in 1.

For his career, Oh has batted .288 and hit 300 home runs.
