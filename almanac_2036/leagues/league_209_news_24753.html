CAL Player of the Week Selected
Monday, September 8th , 2036
Not every ball that came off Ernesto Solorzano's bat last week was a line drive or a towering shot -- it just seemed that way to the teams that played the Inland Empire 66ers. For his exploits, Solorzano was named this week's California League Player of the Week award.

The Inland Empire second baseman put up 10 hits in 26 at-bats and 3 home runs while hitting .385 with 12 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

To date Solorzano has chalked up a .265 average with 16 home runs, driven in 54 runs and scored 71 times.
