Wilder Raps Stockton for 5 Hits
Saturday, August 4th , 2035
There was no storybook ending. David Wilder of the Inland Empire 66ers socked it to the visiting Stockton Ports with a terrific 5-hit game, but his team didn't get the win. The 66ers fell, 15-13, at San Manuel Stadium.

"I don't want to sound ungrateful, but something is missing here," Wilder complained following the loss. "When you have a great game you should win the game, too."

He definitely had an unforgettable day in the batter's box, ripping out 5 hits in 5 at-bats. Inland Empire manager Angelo Ponte sympathized with his third baseman, telling BNN reporters after the game that "David played a whale of a game, but sometimes individual brilliance is not enough."

David Wilder hit a solo-shot off Romtres Taylor in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and walked in the 9th.

Currently Wilder is batting .277 with 14 home runs and 52 RBIs.
