Mercado 5-for-5 for Lakeland
Thursday, April 17th , 2036
Luis Mercado put on a hitting clinic as the Lakeland Flying Tigers dumped the Dunedin Blue Jays, 10-5, at Joker Marchant Stadium.

The Lakeland second baseman made Dunedin defenders earn their pay, lashing Dunedin pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Mercado said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Luis Mercado doubled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit a three-run home run off Wilson Gallego in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Andres Carrizales in the 6th and singled in the 8th.
