Crawdads Get No Resistance from Hot Rods in 13-0 Beat-Down
Wednesday, September 10th , 2036
In a seemingly effortless performance, Alejandro Marulanda of the Hickory Crawdads shot down the Bowling Green Hot Rods, 13-0, on 4 hits.

"Alejandro was just locating so well and using his pitches," Hickory manager Kelvin Nova said later. "He wasn't leaving anything over the plate."

Marulanda recorded 7 strikeouts and 3 walks in a strong outing.

"When he needed to make pitches, he elevated his game," Bowling Green manager Giuseppe Chiaramonte said in postgame comments to BNN. "We couldn't get a key hit off him."

This season, Marulanda has compiled a 13-9 record with a 2.87 ERA in 24 starts.
