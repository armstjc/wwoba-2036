Baltimore-Chicago Deal Confirmed
Monday, July 9th , 2035
After prolonged negotiations Baltimore agreed to ship 25-year old SS Amari Nails and 19-year old minor league LHP Sergio Herrera to Chicago for 27-year old 1B Eric Reiter and 23-year old minor league RHP Chris Spach. The deal is awaiting confirmation from the league office but neither team expects any hang-ups.

"It took longer than expected," said one GM, "but we knew that the deal was going to happen."

Chicago will retain a portion of Reiter's remaining contract.

On the season, Nails is batting .291 with 2 home runs and 25 RBIs. He has played in 74 games.

For the year, Reiter has hit at a .288 clip with 56 RBIs, 11 home runs and 43 runs scored.
