Superb Year for Kim, Wins Most Valuable Player Award
Sunday, October 14th , 2035
At the age of 22, Dong-soo Kim of the Iksan Wiz could very well have his best years ahead of him.

It's a startling thought when you consider the year he had in 2035 was good enough to win the center fielder the Korean Futures League Most Valuable Player Award.

"See the ball, hit the ball," he said. "That's my basic approach."

The top hitter put up a .364 batting average this year, as well as a .464 on-base percentage. He played 98 games and racked up 141 hits, 36 doubles, 1 triple, 6 home runs, 65 RBIs and 75 runs scored.

He received 5 first place votes out of a possible 10. Dong-su Kim of the Icheon Twins finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Dong-hyun Im of the Changwon Dinos finished third with 1 first place vote.
