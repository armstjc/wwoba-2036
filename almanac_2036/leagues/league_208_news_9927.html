Great Stroke: Rivera Gets 5 Hits
Friday, June 15th , 2035
It was almost a perfect day for Esteban Rivera.

The Wisconsin center fielder displayed a hot bat, enjoying an outstanding 5-hit day, but something was missing: The Timber Rattlers lost to the Quad Cities River Bandits, 13-12. Rivera told BNN reporters after the game that luck was "fickle."

Wisconsin manager Marco Yepez told the beat writers otherwise.

"There was no luck today," the skipper said. "He got 5 hits because he has a solid approach to hitting and he put it all together, and we lost because the rest of us didn't put it together so well."

Esteban Rivera singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, doubled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, singled in the 7th, struck out in the 9th and grounded out in the 11th.

To date Rivera has chalked up a .292 average with 7 home runs, driven in 21 runs and scored 47 times.
