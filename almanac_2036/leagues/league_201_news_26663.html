At the young age of only 23, Andres Rodas has been named the International League Reliever of the Year winner for 2036.

The future appears bright for the Norfolk Tides relief ace, who captured the award with 3 saves in 43 relief appearances, 98 strikeouts in 79 innings and a record of 6-1 to go with his 2.85 ERA.

He received 14 first place votes out of a possible 20. Jon Bradley of the Worcester Red Sox finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Alex Clemmey of the Jacksonville Jumbo Shrimp finished third with 1 first place vote.
