Big 3-HR Day for Dowden
Friday, June 27th , 2036
Chris Dowden was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 13-1 Hickory Crawdads triumph at L.P. Frans Stadium.

The 23-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Crawdads past the Rome Braves.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Dowden told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The right fielder went 3-for-4, rung up 4 RBIs and scored 4 runs on his record-setting day.

Chris Dowden hit a two-run home run off Jeff Rusinko in the 1st, walked in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Eric Anderson in the 4th, grounded out in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Tim Benson in the 8th.

Dowden is hitting at a .333 mark this season. His stats show 10 home runs, 24 RBIs and 15 runs scored. He has played 33 games for the Crawdads.
