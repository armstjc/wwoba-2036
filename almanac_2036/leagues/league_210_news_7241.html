Phillips Slams 5 Hits for Augusta
Saturday, April 28th , 2035
It seemed that Augusta center fielder Danny Phillips could do no wrong as the GreenJackets KO'd the Woodpeckers, 16-2. Phillips rapped out 5 hits in 6 at-bats and seemed to know which pitches Woodpeckers hurlers were throwing.

"I think the GreenJackets were stealing signs," growled one unhappy Fayetteville fan after the game.

"I felt good. I saw the ball well and just wanted to make good contact and put the ball in play," said the jubilant Phillips in the postgame press interview.

Danny Phillips struck out in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Samarion Rhamy in the 2nd, tripled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and hit a three-run home run off Daniel Montalvo in the 8th.

Batting .364 to date, Phillips has 5 home runs and 12 RBIs.
