Round Rock Defeats Salt Lake
Friday, October 3rd , 2036
Sometimes the winner is the one who got the lucky bounces and the lucky calls, and sometimes the winner is just the one who played better.

It was the latter case in 2036, as the Round Rock Express topped the Salt Lake Bees 11-2 tonight at Smith's Ballpark to win the Round 1. It was the 2nd title in franchise history.

"When you play for championships, you have to play better than the team in the other dugout. We did that in this series, and all year. Now we're the best team in baseball," said Round Rock catcher Sergio Ontiveros, who was named series MVP.

The Express took the series 4-3. The new titleholders completed the regular season with a first-place finish in the East Division and a 81-69 record.

Round Rock manager Gregory Yersich grinned and nodded when asked to comment on Ontiveros's words.

"Sergio said it best -- we played better than the other team," the skipper said.

Hometown officials announced they were already planning a "grand celebration" for the winners in the days to come.
