NWL Presents Outstanding Pitcher Award
Saturday, September 29th , 2035
Tri-City ace Alexis Cervantes put up some sensational stats for the 2035 season and has been voted the Northwest League Outstanding Pitcher Award winner.

The 22-year-old Dust Devils star made 25 starts and racked up a 10-5 record. He pitched 146.1 innings, collected 123 strikeouts, gave up 30 walks and 136 hits in holding the opposition to a .240 batting average.

He received 6 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Ramiro Orantes of the Spokane Indians finished second in voting, while Eric Coleman of the Spokane Indians finished third.
