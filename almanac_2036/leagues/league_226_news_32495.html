Tarpons Advance to Round 2
Thursday, September 27th , 2035
Tampa first baseman Domingo Castro couldn't hide his reaction when asked about being named MVP of the Round 1.

"It's a nice honor," he said, failing to suppress a wide smile. The trophy was presented to Castro moments after his club eliminated the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels to advance to the Round 2.

Tampa won the final game by a score of 6-5 at George M. Steinbrenner Field and took the series 4-1.

"We won the series and that's the main thing," Castro said. "They were a tough team and we didn't want to take anything for granted."

For the series, Castro hit .353 with a .450 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 4 RBI and scored 3 runs.

"He was tough to stop and that's a big reason they're there and we're here," said a dejected Fort Myers manager Jim Parque.

The Tampa Tarpons will play against the Clearwater Threshers in the next round. Clearwater won their series against the Daytona Tortugas 4 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
