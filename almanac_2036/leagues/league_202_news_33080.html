Mike Good said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 23 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the Round Rock Express rising star has won the Pacific Coast League Reliever of the Year for 2035.

He captured the award on the strength of his 3.65 ERA over 49.1 innings. He also had 34 saves in 49 relief appearances, 47 strikeouts and only 20 walks.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 10. Juan Rubiano of the Salt Lake Bees finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Chris Smrekar of the Las Vegas Aviators finished third.
