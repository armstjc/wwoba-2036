Magana Shines in Winning 2035 Major League Baseball HR Challenge
Monday, July 9th , 2035
Some premier power hitters put on a show at this year's Home Run Challenge. David Magana won the event, which took place at Rogers Centre. To get it done, he had to defeat Joe Atwood in the final round. He did get it done, outscoring Atwood 17-16.

"I paced myself throughout," said Magana. "It helped me get through that last round."

During his career, Magana has chalked up 763 hits and a .284 average with 166 home runs and 448 RBIs.
