3-HR Binge for Castro Against Dunedin
Friday, August 10th , 2035
Tampa Tarpons third baseman Domingo Castro slugged three home runs against the Dunedin Blue Jays at George M. Steinbrenner Field, but couldn't win the game all by himself. The Blue Jays spoiled his day by defeating the Tarpons, 8-6.

"The loss puts a damper on the outstanding game Domingo had, but people will be buzzing about this for days to come," Tampa manager Jonathan Mota. "That's gotta be some consolation to him. But knowing Domingo, he would have preferred a win."

For the game, Castro was 3-for-3 with 4 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

"The first two I got all of them and it was no doubt they were gone," Castro said later. "It seemed like the wind helped it a little on that last one. I just wish Mother Nature had helped us win the game, too."

Domingo Castro hit a two-run home run off Chad Bilotti in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Chad Bilotti in the 3rd, walked in the 5th and hit a solo-shot off Rodolfo Burgos in the 8th.

This season, Castro is batting at a .249 clip with 18 home runs and 66 RBIs.
