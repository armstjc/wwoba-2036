Cedar Rapids Wins Round 1
Wednesday, September 24th , 2036
Fans at Neuroscience Group Field at Fox Cities Stadium saw the Round 1 come to a close today, as the Cedar Rapids Kernels defeated the Wisconsin Timber Rattlers by a score of 12-0, and advanced to the Round 2.

Cedar Rapids won the series 4-1 and will now continue their march toward the Round 2.

"It feels good to be moving on, I can't deny it," said first baseman Josh Quinnie, who was named series MVP. "Not everyone thought we'd make it this far."

The Cedar Rapids Kernels don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Dayton Dragons - Fort Wayne TinCaps series. Fort Wayne leads the series 3 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
