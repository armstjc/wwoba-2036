Midland Starter Wins June's Pitcher Award
Sunday, July 1st , 2035
It was a monstrous June for the Texas League Pitcher of the Month award winner, Juan Hinojosa of the Midland RockHounds. He put up some mighty scary numbers.

Hinojosa recorded 4 wins and no losses in 5 starts. Throwing a total of 37.1 innings, he yielded 38 hits, mowed down 24 on strikes, gave up 15 walks and put up a solid ERA of 1.69 in a stellar month on the mound.

To this point in the season, Hinojosa shows a 9-3 record, 15 starts, 100 innings, 66 strikeouts and a 3.42 ERA.
