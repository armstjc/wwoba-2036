Jae-hoon Kim Captures KBO Award
Monday, September 10th , 2035
With some great hand-eye coordination and a sweet, compact swing, NC Dinos third baseman Jae-hoon Kim took the Korean Baseball Organization by storm this past week to take the latest Player of the Week honors.

"The ball was looking like a beach ball all week," a happy Kim said to BNN reporters. "When it's that big, it doesn't matter where they throw it -- I can hit it anywhere."

The 26-year-old third baseman hit .476 (10-for-21) last week with 2 home runs, 10 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

For the year, Kim is batting .309 with 15 home runs, 64 RBIs and 70 runs scored.
