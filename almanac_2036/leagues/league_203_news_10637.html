Richmond's Wentzell Injured, Out of Lineup
Tuesday, June 26th , 2035
The doctors have spoken and Harmon Wentzell of the Richmond Flying Squirrels didn't like what he heard. The 26-year-old second baseman is almost certainly gone for the rest of the year and depending on his progress could miss some of next season as well. Wentzell sustained his season-ending injury during the Altoona Curve game yesterday at People's Natural Gas Field. He had to leave the game because he hurt himself while running the bases. Today the Richmond medical staff reported Wentzell had a torn posterior cruciate ligament and would be absent from the lineup for at least 10 months.

This year, Wentzell is batting .269 with 3 home runs and 8 RBIs.
