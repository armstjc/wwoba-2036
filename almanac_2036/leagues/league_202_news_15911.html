Trinidad Crushes 4 Home Runs
Saturday, April 5th , 2036
It was a fairy-tale day for Jorge Trinidad -- right up until the end.

The Oklahoma City Dodgers third baseman had an almost-unbelievable 4 home runs at Southwest University park, but his fairy-tale ending was snatched away by the El Paso Chihuahuas, who grabbed the win, 12-11.

"I'm paid to hit 'em, and I hit 'em real good today, but not good enough to win the game," Trinidad said to BNN reporters after his historic game. "The loss tarnishes it a bit and takes away some from the day. Give it to the Chihuahuas, they hung in and stayed with us the whole time."

On his big day, the third baseman for the Dodgers had 4 hits in 5 at-bats, 8 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

"Jorge probably earned himself a few days without hearing any criticism from me," Oklahoma City manager Sergio De Luna told reporters later. "The rest of the guys, not so much."

Jorge Trinidad struck out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Juan Serrano in the 3rd, hit a three-run home run off Juan Serrano in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Chris Alexander in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Chris Alexander in the 7th and walked in the 9th.

Trinidad is hitting .310 this year with 6 home runs, 11 RBIs and 7 runs scored. He has 9 hits in 29 at-bats, 2 walks and a .375 on-base percentage. Trinidad has played in 7 games.
