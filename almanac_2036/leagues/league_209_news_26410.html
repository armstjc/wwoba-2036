CAL Manager of the Year Award Goes to Motley
Thursday, October 16th , 2036
Fresno manager Brittan Motley caused quite a stir when he came to town. Noted for his feisty, no-nonsense attitude, Motley threw his all into the 2036 campaign, winning over critics and fans as he went. He also won a few ballgames, running up a 91-41 record, which was good enough to take the first place spot in the Northern Division standings.

"These guys are grown men. They don't need psychobabble, they need to win ballgames," Motley snapped at his first press conference. While his approach was controversial, even for Fresno, it netted the 57-year-old the California League Manager of the Year trophy. In response to which he replied, "It ain't molecular physics, it's baseball," and began discussing potential free agents with the local media.
