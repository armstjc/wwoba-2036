Kim No-Hits Gyeongsan
Friday, April 13th , 2035
Dong-hwan Kim relied on excellent pitch selection and pinpoint control to hold Gyeongsan hitless in a win, 3-0, at Daegu Samsung Lions Park.

"Nobody goes out to pitch a no-hitter," he said later. "We just go out and try to get a win. A 10-9 win is better than a 1-0 loss. My pitching buddies might not be too happy giving up nine runs but a win is a win. The key to it today was I mixed my pitches well. I kept the Lions off balance and guessing the whole game."

The 20-year-old starter rung up 11 strikeouts and 1 walk in whipping the Lions.

"When a guy is throwing strikes the way he was and is locating well, it is going to be a long day for any club," Gyeongsan skipper Seo-jin Kang said.
