Fightin Phils Armendariz Lost for Year
Wednesday, June 4th , 2036
Doctors in Reading said Juan Armendariz might not play anymore this year. The Fightin Phils starting pitcher suffered a torn meniscus during a game against the Portland Sea Dogs and will be out for at least 4-5 months. Club officials told BNN they do not expect him to return to the team this season.

Currently Armendariz has a 4-1 won-lost record in 10 starts with a 5.51 ERA. He also has 38 strikeouts in 49 innings.
