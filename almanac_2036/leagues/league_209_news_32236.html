Lake Elsinore Moves on to Round 2
Friday, September 21st , 2035
Lake Elsinore right fielder Edgar Padilla couldn't hide his reaction when asked about being named MVP of the Round 1.

"It's a nice honor," he said, failing to suppress a wide smile. The trophy was presented to Padilla moments after his club eliminated the Inland Empire 66ers to advance to the Round 2.

Lake Elsinore won the final game by a score of 4-3 at Lake Elsinore Diamond and took the series 4-2.

"We won the series and that's the main thing," Padilla said. "They were a tough team and we didn't want to take anything for granted."

For the series, Padilla hit .320 with a .433 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 4 runs.

"He was tough to stop and that's a big reason they're there and we're here," said a dejected Inland Empire manager Angelo Ponte.

The Lake Elsinore Storm don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Modesto Nuts - Fresno Grizzlies series. Fresno leads the series 3 games to 2. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
