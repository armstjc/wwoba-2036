Hanwha Beats NC in Korean Series
Wednesday, October 24th , 2035
The Hanwha faithful were cheering in the streets, and NC fans wiping away the tears, after the Eagles won the Korean Series at Masan Baseball Stadium today.

The Eagles clinched it by knocking off the Dinos 3-1 to take their 3rd Korean Baseball Organization crown. Hanwha took the series 4-1.

"This club never gave up all year long," Hanwha manager John Wesley told the press. "They believed they could win the title and they did."

On the way to the Korean Series title, Hanwha compiled a 79-61 won-loss record and finished first in the Korean Baseball Organization.

"We just didn't play well enough to win," Dinos manager Joon-yong Chae said. "We just didn't make the big plays like they did."
