Hwangbo Pitches Gem as Giants Beat KIA, 8-0
Saturday, August 2nd , 2036
Lotte Giants starter Joo-hyun Hwangbo really did a number on the KIA Tigers, shutting them out, 8-0, on 1 hit in a complete game victory. He issued 1 walk and had 8 strikeouts.

"We didn't have good at-bats," said KIA manager Joo-sung Jang. "I don't make excuses, but when we were playing the game, our energy didn't show. That comes from either extremely good pitching or extremely lousy hitting and seeing as our team has a bunch of good hitters, I have to tip my cap to Hwangbo."

In 11 starts this year, Hwangbo has posted 4 wins while suffering 5 losses. He has pitched 64 innings, allowed 48 hits, struck out 51 and has a 3.66 ERA.
