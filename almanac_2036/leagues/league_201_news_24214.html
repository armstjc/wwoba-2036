Kummet Goes Downtown 3 Times Against Red Sox
Sunday, August 24th , 2036
Anyone wearing a glove and sitting in the bleachers for today's Mets-Red Sox game had a good chance at snagging a home run ball.

Syracuse right fielder Eric Kummet thrice sent eager fans scurrying to catch a souvenir, helping lead the Syracuse to a win, 13-2, over Worcester.

"Hey, the three homers were great, but I'm just trying to help my ballclub," Kummet said later. "I'm not going out there thinking I need another home run."

Kummet, who is tied for 5th in the in extra base hits with 63, was 4-for-4 on the day with 6 runs batted in.

Eric Kummet hit a solo-shot off David Ascencio in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off David Ascencio in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Juan Borrego in the 7th and walked in the 8th.

In 97 games Kummet is hitting .288 this season with 104 hits, 39 home runs, 94 RBIs, 58 walks, .397 OBP and has scored 84 runs.
