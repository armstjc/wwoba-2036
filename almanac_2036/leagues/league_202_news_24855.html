Fricke Belts Bunch of Base Hits
Tuesday, September 9th , 2036
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Jonathan Fricke of the Reno Aces. Fricke went on a tear at Greater Nevada Field and cracked hit after hit off Las Vegas pitching. When the dust settled on the diamond, the 23-year-old left fielder had a career 5-for-6 day and something he will long remember.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but I don't think I'll tell them we lost," he joked in the BNN postgame interview.

Despite Fricke's personal accolades, the Las Vegas Aviators walked off the field with the 13-10 win.

Jonathan Fricke singled in the 1st, doubled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Luis del Valle in the 5th, singled in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

This year, Fricke is batting .314 with 8 home runs and 50 RBIs.
