KBO Hands Out Reliever of the Year
Saturday, October 25th , 2036
Sung-min Hwang was a calming influence out of the Lotte Giants bullpen this season.

His teammates and manager said he had a poised demeanor about him that was typical of someone much older and more experienced. His poise has paid off, as the 25-year-old turned in a sparkling season and has been named the winner of the Korean Baseball Organization Reliever of the Year for 2036.

The Yongin, South Korea native won the award with 43 saves in 55 relief appearances, 67 strikeouts in 56 innings and a record of 0-2 to go with his 1.77 ERA.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 10. Dong-su Kim of the LG Twins finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Kyu-soo Kim of the Hanwha Eagles finished third.
