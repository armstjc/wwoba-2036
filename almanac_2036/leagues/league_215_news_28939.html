Nationals End Alaimo Streak
Sunday, July 22nd , 2035
The 21-game hitting streak of New York A (FCL) Yankees second baseman Mike Alaimo came to an end at The Ballpark of the Palm Beaches. The Washington (FCL) Nationals held him hitless in 4 at-bats and also sent the Yankees down to defeat, 5-2.

Later, Alaimo acknowledged to BNN reporters that the hit streak meant something to him.

"It was special," he said, "but I think other people make a bigger deal out of it than I do, or the players do. It was what we're all supposed to do; go out and hit the ball and try to score runs. It was a cool deal, but it's over now. Let's try to start a new one."

For the year, Alaimo is batting .291 with 7 home runs, 22 RBIs and 18 runs scored.
