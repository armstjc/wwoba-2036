Round 1 Goes the Distance, Rocket City Prevails
Sunday, September 30th , 2035
Mark one up for the Rocket City Trash Pandas.

In the crucial final game, they put away the Birmingham Barons 4-3 at Toyota Field to win the Round 1, 4-3 and advance to the Round 2.

Although his team came up short, Birmingham's Edgar Noriega was named the series MVP, with many of the league media feeling the series would have been over much sooner if not for his contributions.

"Noriega did a lot to keep Birmingham in it, no doubt about that," said Trash Pandas manager Bill Kerry.

The Rocket City Trash Pandas will play against the Pensacola Blue Wahoos in the next round. Pensacola won their series against the Montgomery Biscuits 4 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
