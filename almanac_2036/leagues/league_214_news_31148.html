San Francisco (ACL) Starter Wins August's Pitcher Award
Saturday, September 1st , 2035
The August Pitcher of the Month has been chosen and the Arizona Complex League award went to San Francisco (ACL) hurler, Josh Burtner.

During the month, Burtner was impressive with a 1.05 ERA and 2 wins and 1 loss. In 34.1 innings he struck out 37 and gave up 17 hits and 9 walks.

To date, Burtner has a 5-5 mark in 13 starts with a 1.92 ERA. He has given up 74 hits in 98.1 innings, struck out 108 and issued 40 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .209 batting average against him.
