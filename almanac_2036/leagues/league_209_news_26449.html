Villarreal Scores CAL Best Hurler Honors
Friday, October 17th , 2036
The 2036 California League Outstanding Pitcher Award winner is Daniel Villarreal of the Fresno Grizzlies.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Romtres Taylor of the Stockton Ports finished second in voting, while Jim Burnett of the Visalia Rawhide finished third.
