Flying Squirrels Gulbranson Gone for Year
Thursday, June 21st , 2035
Richmond starting pitcher Chris Gulbranson could miss the remainder of the season because of shoulder inflammation. He exited yesterday's Binghamton game after injuring himself while throwing a pitch. The original diagnosis said Gulbranson would be out 8 months. Later on his doctors said he could be out even longer. "He's done," his manager commented at the BNN press conference. "It's a season-ending injury, but it could have been worse. It's not the worst-case scenario, so we're happy about that."

Up to now Gulbranson has recorded a record of 6-5. In 14 starts he has fanned 83 batters in 99.1 innings with a 2.08 ERA.
