Jacksonville Tops Scranton WB
Saturday, October 11th , 2036
With a 5-3 win over the Scranton WB RailRiders at 121 Financial Park today, the Jacksonville Jumbo Shrimp wrapped up their 1st title in team history.

The Jumbo Shrimp left little doubt who was the better team. Celebrating a banner year, "team" was the word of the day in the victorious clubhouse after the game.

"Hitting, pitching, fielding; those are all things you need to win, but most importantly you need a team, a team that works together toward the same collective goal," said Jacksonville manager Mike Johns. "Our players felt that way all year, and they played that way. When one guy had an off day, 24 others stepped up their game and filled the void."

A jovial mood filled the air as Jacksonville celebrated into the night. The city's mayor said parade plans were underway and a "huge celebration" would be in order.

"I hope they have a big celebration," Scranton WB manager Jay Sullenger said. "They deserved to win this series and they deserve to celebrate it."
