Dinos Move on to Korean Series
Wednesday, October 17th , 2035
The NC Dinos are off to the Korean Series.

By a final score of 13-2 at Masan Baseball Stadium, NC ended the Playoff and knocked out the Samsung Lions by a series score of 3-1.

"Two great teams, only one can go on and we're happy that's us," said Kang-il Kim, who was named series MVP. "There's no easy games, no easy series in the Korean Baseball Organization playoffs."

For the series, Kim hit .500 with a .579 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 7 runs.

The Korean Series matchups and schedule will be announced after the Playoff finishes.
