Royals Advance to League Championship Series
Friday, October 17th , 2036
The Kansas City Royals are heading to the League Championship Series, having narrowly defeated the New York Yankees.

Kansas City edged New York in the series 3-2, with a win at Yankee Stadium in the clinching game by a 7-3 margin.

Steve Foster turned in a strong offensive performance for the Royals and got series MVP for his efforts.

"I'll never turn down another trophy for my mantle, another accomplishment to remember once my career is over," he said. "What I would really remember, though, is a World Series win."

It remains to be seen how the playoffs will conclude, but if Foster continues to swing a hot bat, his team will no doubt have a chance.

For the series, he hit .350 with a .350 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, 7 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The Kansas City Royals will play against the Toronto Blue Jays in the next round. Toronto won their series against the Oakland Athletics 3 games to 1. The League Championship Series is scheduled to begin on October 18th , 2036.
