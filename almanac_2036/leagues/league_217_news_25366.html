Lions Seung-min Ko Will Miss Season
Friday, September 19th , 2036
Samsung first baseman Seung-min Ko could miss the remainder of the season because of a fractured knee. He exited yesterday's Hanwha game after injuring himself while running the bases. The original diagnosis said Ko would be out 5 months. Later on his doctors said he could be out even longer. "He's done," his manager commented at the BNN press conference. "It's a season-ending injury, but it could have been worse. It's not the worst-case scenario, so we're happy about that."

So far this year in 68 games, Ko is hitting .256 with 6 home runs, 17 RBIs and 18 runs scored.
