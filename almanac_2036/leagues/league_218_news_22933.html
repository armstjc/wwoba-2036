Seosan-Changwon Fight Results in Suspensions
Saturday, August 2nd , 2036
Hyun-woo Kim of the Seosan Eagles and Dong-hoon Kim of the Changwon Dinos will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 2 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Eagles-Dinos game at Changwon Baseball Training Stadium.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Kim and Kim will begin serving them soon.

This season, Kim is hitting at a .282 pace with 2 home runs, 33 RBIs and has scored 37 times.

Checking out his stats for the season, Kim has notched 4 wins and 5 losses in 16 starts. His ERA is 6.08 and he's struck out 51 batters in 74 innings.
