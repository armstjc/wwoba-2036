ACL Manager of the Year Award Goes to Flores
Tuesday, September 16th , 2036
Texas (ACL) Rangers field manager Jose Flores has brought a winning attitude to the team and the city. His no-nonsense approach, work ethic and baseball experience have paved the way for success.

This season the Rangers parlayed his managerial skills and some talented ballplayers into a second place spot in the Arizona Complex League East standings. Texas (ACL) finished the year with a 42-20 record.

To everybody's delight Flores was voted the 2036 Arizona Complex League Manager of the Year by the baseball writers.

"Mr. Flores doesn't take any nonsense. If you aren't doing your job, he's not afraid to call you on it," noted one of his ballplayers. "But the team wouldn't want to play for anyone else. He's tough, demanding, but he treats everyone fairly and doesn't play favorites. Nobody gets pampered on his ballclub."
