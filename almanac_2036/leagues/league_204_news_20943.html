Kearns Is SL's Best Pitcher in June
Tuesday, July 1st , 2036
A reliever is at his best when the game is on the line. The pressure doesn't get to him. Juan Kearns of the Chattanooga Lookouts is just that kind of pitcher. In June, he was acclaimed the Pitcher of the Month by the Southern League.

Chattanooga's star reliever held the opposition to a .186 average and put up 8 saves and 13 strikeouts in 11.2 innings with a 2-2 mark and a 2.31 ERA.

This year, his season stats show Kearns has 9 saves in 15 relief appearances with a 3-2 mark and 1.93 ERA.
