NC's Choi to Retire
Thursday, July 3rd , 2036
Ji-gwang Choi said he's looking forward to getting home to Busan, South Korea to relax and unwind.

At age 38, the NC Dinos player has announced he will retire at the end of the season.

"I'm thankful for my time in Korean Baseball Organization, but all good things must come to an end," he said.

On his career ledger Choi has a 24-22 mark, 308 appearances, 5 saves and a 4.44 ERA. He has 395 strikeouts in 436 innings pitched.
