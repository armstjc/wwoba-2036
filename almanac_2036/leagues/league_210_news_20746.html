Hit Parade: Diller Gets Cycle
Thursday, June 26th , 2036
Most fans have never seen a player hit for the cycle, and most players have never done it -- but it happened today at Segra Park. Jeff Diller of the Columbia Fireflies accomplished the feat against Lynchburg Hillcats.

Diller went 4-for-5 with 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run in the 11-3 Columbia victory.

"That was one for the grandkids, am I right?" Diller said after the game.

Diller also scored 2 runs and had 3 RBI in a day to remember for both Diller and the crowd at Segra Park.

Jeff Diller tripled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Arturo Banda in the 6th and hit an RBI double in the 7th.

In 63 games this season, Diller has batted at a .283 pace with 76 hits and 5 home runs.
