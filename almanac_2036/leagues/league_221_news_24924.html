Rivera Smacks 5 Hits for Twins
Wednesday, September 10th , 2036
Egiodeo Rivera was at the head of a Minnesota (DSL) hit parade at Baseball City Complex. The third baseman went to the plate 5 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the Twins to a nice 13-4 victory over the Arizona (DSL) Diamondbacks.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Rivera told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

Diamondbacks skipper B.J. Leach said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Egiodeo Rivera singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, doubled in the 4th, doubled in the 6th, walked in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

Thus far this season in 50 games, Rivera is batting .323 with 13 home runs, 40 RBIs and 60 runs scored. He has 62 hits in 192 at-bats.
