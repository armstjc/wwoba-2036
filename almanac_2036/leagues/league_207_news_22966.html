Groff Selected Player of the Week
Monday, August 4th , 2036
Jersey Shore fans have something to crow about today as Jersey Shore right fielder Billy Groff nabbed the South Atlantic League Player of the Week Award for his performance over the last seven days.

"I know there are times when it's hard to lay off pitches. Everyone goes through stretches when he's too eager to put the ball in play. You know, though, I feel like this past week I was able to get in the right frame of mind and just let things come naturally," said Groff.

He posted a .500 average (11-for-22) this past week with 3 home runs, 13 RBIs, and 8 runs scored.

This season, Groff is batting .299 with 17 home runs. He has scored 75 runs and driven in 65.
