Blomquist Cracks 3 HR for Tarpons
Thursday, June 5th , 2036
Whatever Andy Blomquist ate for breakfast before his game against the Bradenton Marauders, it gave him some impressive power.

The Tampa catcher smacked three balls up and out of the park at George M. Steinbrenner Field. His home run barrage helped the Tarpons defeat the Marauders, 12-0.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 3-for-5, scored 3 times, drove in 5 and put on a display that history will remember.

Andy Blomquist hit a solo-shot off Jody Griffin in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Jody Griffin in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Nelson Maldonado in the 5th, struck out in the 7th and flied out in the 8th.

Blomquist is batting .255 this season in 39 games with 5 home runs, 16 RBIs and 14 runs scored. He has 35 hits in 137 at-bats.
