League Suspends Miami (FCL) LF Rivera
Tuesday, June 24th , 2036
The Miami (FCL) Marlins were not very happy with the home plate umpire in today's loss to Minnesota (FCL) at Roger Dean Stadium. One in particular -- Edgar Rivera. Upon being called out on strikes, the Marlins left fielder gave the ump quite an earful. For that, the man in blue gave him the thumb and sent him to an early shower.

But the disgruntled Rivera lingered on the field for another five minutes, berating the umpire. Finally his manager got him off the field and into the clubhouse.

But it got worse after the game, when Rivera told reporters, "He was awful out there. They played 'Three Blind Mice' for him."

For his escapades and after-the-game oration, the league office suspended Rivera for 4 games.

Currently Rivera is batting .308 with 4 hits and no home runs. He has knocked in 2 runs and scored 2 times.
