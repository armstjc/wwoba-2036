League Championship Series Goes the Distance, Philadelphia Prevails
Monday, October 22nd , 2035
The Philadelphia Phillies and Atlanta Braves held nothing back in an entertaining League Championship Series that went the distance.

In the end, it will be Philadelphia advancing to the World Series, after a 3-2 triumph in the final game at Citizens Bank Park, to wrap up the series 4-3.

The Phillies players celebrated jubilantly in their clubhouse after the game, while the scene was one of dismay on the opposing side for the Braves.

Chad Curtis was named series MVP for Philadelphia. For the series, Curtis hit .333 with a .440 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The Philadelphia Phillies will play against the Oakland Athletics in the next round. Oakland won their series against the Kansas City Royals 4 games to 2. The World Series is scheduled to begin on October 23rd , 2035.
