Despite his best efforts, game MVP Josh Quinnie's West All-Stars couldn't overcome the powerful East squad in the annual All-Star Game.

The East All-Stars triumphed in the game by a final score of 5-3 in this year's game at Veterans Memorial Stadium.

During his career, Quinnie has chalked up 140 hits and a .297 average with 30 home runs and 105 RBIs.
