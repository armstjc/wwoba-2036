Jersey Shore Wins Round 1
Thursday, September 20th , 2035
The Jersey Shore BlueClaws are off to the Round 2.

By a final score of 3-2 at Leidos Field at Ripken Stadium, Jersey Shore ended the Round 1 and knocked out the Aberdeen Ironbirds by a series score of 4-1.

"Two great teams, only one can go on and we're happy that's us," said Luis Galindo, who was named series MVP. "There's no easy games, no easy series in the South Atlantic League playoffs."

For the series, Galindo hit .300 with a .300 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 7 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Jersey Shore BlueClaws don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Rome Braves - Hickory Crawdads series. Rome leads the series 3 games to 2. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
