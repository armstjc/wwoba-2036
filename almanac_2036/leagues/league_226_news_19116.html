Florida State League's Best Batter Announced
Monday, May 26th , 2036
Not every ball that came off Ben Searles's bat last week was a line drive or a towering shot -- it just seemed that way to the teams that played the Daytona Tortugas. For his exploits, Searles was named this week's Florida State League Player of the Week award.

The Daytona third baseman put up 12 hits in 25 at-bats and no home runs while hitting .480 with 5 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

So far this season, Searles has gotten 46 hits in 144 at-bats for a .319 batting average with 9 home runs. His stats include 31 RBIs and 27 runs scored. He has played in 36 games.
