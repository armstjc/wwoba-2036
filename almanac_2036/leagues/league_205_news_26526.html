Reliever of the Year Goes to Friedt
Sunday, October 19th , 2036
He may only be 25, but Kevin Friedt of the Northwest Arkansas Naturals has already established himself as one of the elite relievers in the Texas League.

The reliever was today named the TL Reliever of the Year winner for 2036.

He won the award with a strong year that saw him put up 21 saves in 48 relief appearances and 73 strikeouts in 82.2 innings. He had a record of 10-8 to go with his 2.40 ERA.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Saulito Watson of the San Antonio Missions finished second in voting, while Takumo Ogston of the Frisco RoughRiders finished third.
