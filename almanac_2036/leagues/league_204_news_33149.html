Kerry Takes Top SL Manager Honors
Friday, October 19th , 2035
When Bill Kerry was hired to lead the Rocket City Trash Pandas, fans were expecting improvement over the last manager. Today, flanked by team officials, Kerry accepted the Southern League Manager of the Year Award, noting the level of passion for the Rocket City Trash Pandas.

"The biggest challenge was the fans. I don't know how many times I was asked, if I thought the club was good enough. It was as if they expected failure. It was much easier to win over the ballplayers. Slowly but surely the fans began to believe in us."

Kerry led Rocket City to a first place finish in the North Division. The Trash Pandas ended up with a 74-64 record.

Kerry is looking forward to the offseason. "We're reviewing our options. You never stand still in baseball. You either get better or worse, 'cause everyone else is out to get your free agents or deal for your prospects. It's going to be a challenge." But the skipper has more than proven he is equal to the task.
