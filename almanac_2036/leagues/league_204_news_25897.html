Shuckers Win Round 1
Friday, October 3rd , 2036
The Biloxi Shuckers clubhouse was filled with glee and celebration tonight, while the Montgomery Biscuits room was filled with despair and sadness after the Round 1 concluded.

"Someone's got to win and someone's got to lose, we're just glad we're on the good end of it tonight and can enjoy this," said D.J. Sanders, who was named MVP for a strong series of offensive performance.

Biloxi won the final game by a 8-2 count at MGM Park and the series 4-2.

Montgomery manager Derin McMains was simple and to-the-point in his assessment of the series.

"They were the better team and deserve to move on," he said.

For the series, Sanders hit .429 with a .480 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 7 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Biloxi Shuckers will play against the Chattanooga Lookouts in the next round. Chattanooga won their series against the Rocket City Trash Pandas 4 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
