June's Best NL Hurler is Schneider
Tuesday, July 1st , 2036
The National League Pitcher of the Month honor has been given to Doug Schneider of San Diego for a superlative month.

"I actually never wanted to be a pitcher," Schneider told reporters when asked who he credits for his pitching ability. "I was an outfielder until high school and on the third day of tryouts my coach came up and said, 'Son you couldn't hit a lick even if we put it on a tee for ya.' Well, that ticked me off, so I decided if I couldn't hit I wanted to make sure no one else could either and so here I am."

It all turned out for the best as the San Diego starter notched a 5-0 record with a 1.29 ERA last month. He made 5 starts and struck out 27 in 35 innings.

For the year, Schneider has compiled a 8-5 record with a 2.84 ERA.
