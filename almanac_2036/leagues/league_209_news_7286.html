Stockton's Rivera Snaps Up Player of the Week
Monday, April 30th , 2035
Stockton fans have something to crow about today as Stockton center fielder Alfredo Rivera nabbed the California League Player of the Week Award for his performance over the last seven days.

"I know there are times when it's hard to lay off pitches. Everyone goes through stretches when he's too eager to put the ball in play. You know, though, I feel like this past week I was able to get in the right frame of mind and just let things come naturally," said Rivera.

He posted a .417 average (10-for-24) this past week with 4 home runs, 7 RBIs, and 6 runs scored.

So far this season, Rivera has recorded a .410 batting average with 34 hits, 7 home runs, 16 RBIs, 17 runs scored and a .479 OBP.
