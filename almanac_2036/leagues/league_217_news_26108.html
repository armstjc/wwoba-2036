Wednesday, October 8th , 2036
It was a sweet sweep for the Doosan Bears as they eased past the Lotte Giants to advance to the Korean Series.

Doosan won the final game by a 5-4 count and the series 3-0.

"We're just fortunate to get through it quickly and be able to rest up for the next series," said Joon-suk Kim, who was named series MVP.

For the series, Kim hit .769 with a .786 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 3 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The Korean Series matchups and schedule will be announced after the Playoff finishes.
