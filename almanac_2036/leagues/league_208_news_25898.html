It's Over! Cedar Rapids Defeats Fort Wayne
Friday, October 3rd , 2036
With a 9-2 win over the Fort Wayne TinCaps tonight at Parkview Field, the Cedar Rapids Kernels wrapped up their 3rd title in team history.

Cedar Rapids left little doubt who was the better club. "Team" was the word of the day as the Kernels celebrated the culmination of their banner year in the victors clubhouse following the game.

"Hitting, pitching, fielding; those are all things you need to win, but most importantly you need a team, a team that works together toward the same collective goal," said Cedar Rapids manager Jarret Martin. "Our players felt that way all year and they played that way. When one guy had an off day, 24 others stepped up their game and filled the void."

A jovial mood filled the air as Cedar Rapids celebrated into the night. The city's mayor said parade plans were underway and a "huge celebration" in the works.

"We're going to celebrate our team, our championship Cedar Rapids Kernels," the mayor said.

The champions completed the regular season with a first-place finish in the West and a 86-46 record.
