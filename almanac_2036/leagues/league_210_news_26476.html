CAR Names Outstanding Pitcher Award Winner
Saturday, October 18th , 2036
The Carolina League Outstanding Pitcher Award winner has been chosen and the trophy goes to Charleston hurler Randy Mauro for his outstanding season in 2036.

Mauro was quite impressive with a 13-2 record and a 2.18 ERA. In 27 starts and 169.1 innings, he struck out 154 and gave up 126 hits and 68 walks. Teams hit .207 against him.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 12. Jeff Hardage of the Myrtle Beach Pelicans finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Chris Denny of the Carolina Mudcats finished third.
