KFL's Premier Player: Kim of Iksan
Monday, October 20th , 2036
At the age of 22, Hyo-jun Kim of the Iksan Wiz could very well have his best years ahead of him.

It's a startling thought when you consider the year he had in 2036 was good enough to win the left fielder the Korean Futures League Most Valuable Player Award.

"See the ball, hit the ball," he said. "That's my basic approach."

The top hitter put up a .348 batting average this year, as well as a .479 on-base percentage. He played 68 games and racked up 85 hits, 26 doubles, 4 triples, 8 home runs, 52 RBIs and 45 runs scored.

He received 1 first place votes out of a possible 10. Seung-kyu Park of the Hampyeong Tigers finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Min-han Kim of the Hampyeong Tigers finished third with 1 first place vote.

Best KFL Hurler Chosen for 2036
Sunday, October 19th , 2036
This season's Korean Futures League Outstanding Pitcher Award trophy went to Jin-soo Kim, the ace of the Hampyeong pitching staff.

He dominated all season, finishing with 9 wins and 2 losses and a 3.39 ERA. In 23 starts he worked a total of 114 innings, striking out 118, while walking 64. Other clubs managed to hit only .250 against him.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 10. Gyu-hyun Kim of the Sangdong Giants finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Young-min Yoo of the Changwon Dinos finished third.
