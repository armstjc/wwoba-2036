5-for-5 Day for Kwon
Tuesday, September 4th , 2035
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Sa-yoon Kwon is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-5 against the SSG Landers at Daegu Samsung Lions Park. The monster effort from the 25-year-old second baseman helped the Samsung Lions get the win, 10-4.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Landers manager Si-hoon Park. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Sa-yoon Kwon hit a three-run home run off Myeong-seong Jeon in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Joon-young Kim in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off Joon-young Kim in the 7th.

Looking at his season stats, Kwon has compiled a .318 batting average and a .411 on-base percentage, while hitting 19 home runs and driving in 76 runs.
