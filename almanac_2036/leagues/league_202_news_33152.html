2035 PCL Outstanding Pitcher Award Named
Friday, October 19th , 2035
The Pacific Coast League announced today that Gregorio Romero has been chosen the Outstanding Pitcher Award winner for 2035. The Round Rock starter had everything working for him this past season as he soared to a 13-6 record.

In his exceptional season Romero chalked up a 3.52 ERA in 184.1 innings with 159 strikeouts and 80 walks. Enemy teams hit .222 against him in his 28 starts.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 10. Dan Williams of the Round Rock Express finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Dan McDonald of the Sugar Land Space Cowboys finished third.
