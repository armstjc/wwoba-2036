Ironbirds Luis Pillado Swings Hot Bat, SAL Winner
Monday, August 18th , 2036
Aberdeen fans have something to crow about today as Aberdeen shortstop Luis Pillado nabbed the South Atlantic League Player of the Week Award for his performance over the last seven days.

"I know there are times when it's hard to lay off pitches. Everyone goes through stretches when he's too eager to put the ball in play. You know, though, I feel like this past week I was able to get in the right frame of mind and just let things come naturally," said Pillado.

He posted a .391 average (9-for-23) this past week with 4 home runs, 7 RBIs, and 6 runs scored.

Pillado on the season is batting .308 with 15 home runs. His totals include 47 RBIs and 80 runs scored. He has played in 94 games.
