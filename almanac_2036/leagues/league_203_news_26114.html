Cavalcanti Grabs EL Reliever of the Year
Thursday, October 9th , 2036
At the age of 24, Juan Cavalcanti says he still has a lot to learn about life in Eastern League.

But he certainly seems to be a quick study, as the Binghamton Rumble Ponies reliever was better than all others this year and has captured the 2036 Eastern League Reliever of the Year.

"I hope to win this a few more times before all is said and done in my career," the
Catia La Mar, Venezuela native said.

For the season, Cavalcanti had 35 saves in 49 relief appearances, which totaled 56.1. He fanned 47 betters, while walking 25 and put up a record of 2-4 to go with his 2.08 ERA.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Jim Blakes of the Reading Fightin Phils finished second in voting, while Jorge Moncada of the Somerset Patriots finished third.
