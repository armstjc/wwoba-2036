Kim on Fire: 5-for-6 for Gyeongsan
Friday, September 5th , 2036
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Deuk-jin Kim of the Gyeongsan Lions. Kim went on a tear at Daegu Samsung Lions Park and cracked hit after hit off Icheon pitching. When the dust settled on the diamond, the 20-year-old first baseman had a career 5-for-6 day and something he will long remember.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but I don't think I'll tell them we lost," he joked in the BNN postgame interview.

Despite Kim's personal accolades, the Icheon Bears walked off the field with the 15-10 win.

Deuk-jin Kim singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, grounded out in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

This year, Kim has hit at a .273 pace and produced 41 RBIs, 5 home runs and scored 45 runs.
