Texas-Toronto Fight Results in Suspensions
Tuesday, August 28th , 2035
Sam Philbrick of the Texas Rangers and George Kirby of the Toronto Blue Jays will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 2 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Rangers-Blue Jays game at Rogers Centre.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Philbrick and Kirby will begin serving them soon.

So far this season, Philbrick is batting .251 with 12 home runs, 39 RBIs and 63 runs scored. He has 111 hits in 443 at-bats, 40 walks and a .322 OBP. Philbrick has played in 123 games.

On his stats sheet this season, Kirby shows a 9-12 record, 26 starts, 153.1 innings, 133 strikeouts and a 3.93 ERA.
