Oakland Reliever September's Best Pitcher
Monday, October 1st , 2035
When Chris Rodriguez strolls in from the bullpen, he is a good bet to nail down the victory for the Oakland Athletics. That's the way it was last month as he performed his job in an almost flawless fashion. Today he was honored as September's Pitcher of the Month by the American League.

The Oakland pitcher compiled a 4-0 record, 4 saves, 9 strikeouts and a 2.31 ERA in 11.2 innings over 11 relief appearances.

This year, Rodriguez has made 57 relief appearances with 8 saves. In 69.2 innings, he has permitted 60 hits, fanned 86 hitters and recorded 9 wins and 1 loss with a 2.07 ERA.
