Thursday, June 28th , 2035
After an intense confrontation, both the Bears Joon-suk Kim and the Landers Myeong-seong Jeon were handed their punishments today. "Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Joon-suk Kim commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."
