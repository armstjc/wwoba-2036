Florida Complex League Names League's Best
Friday, September 19th , 2036
Chris Munro took Florida Complex League by storm this year, and he did it all at the age of 22.

For his spectacular campaign, the Minnesota (FCL) Twins star has been named the 2036 Florida Complex League Most Valuable Player Award recipient.

"To be considered amongst the best in the league is overwhelming," said the left fielder.

On the season he compiled a .377 batting average to go with a .486 on-base percentage. The native of Silverdale, Washington also added 22 doubles, 8 triples, 15 home runs, 55 RBIs and 51 runs scored.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 15. Josh Medina of the Philadelphia (FCL) Phillies finished second in voting with 6 first place votes, while Alpha Marbley of the Philadelphia (FCL) Phillies finished third.
