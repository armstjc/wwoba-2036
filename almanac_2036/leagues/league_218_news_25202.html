Kim on Fire, 5-for-5 Against Iksan
Tuesday, September 16th , 2036
It was a sensational day at the plate for Icheon Bears center fielder Dong-hyun Kim, but a tough one for the team, as the Bears dropped the contest, 8-4, to the Iksan Wiz.

Even with the loss, Kim was pure misery for the Iksan pitching staff. He left them shell-shocked after going 5-for-5 in an explosive display of hitting.

"It's always good to get a bunch of hits in a game, but you also want to win," said Kim, who is the 22-year old center fielder .

"Kim really hit the ball hard and when you do that, you've got a good chance to do some damage," Wiz manager Sun-myung Choi told BNN reporters after the game. "I feel like we threw him some good pitches, but he just put some good swings on the ball."

Dong-hyun Kim singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Ki-moon Kim in the 7th and hit a two-run double in the 8th.

This season in 11 games, Kim is batting .500 with 1 home run, 8 RBIs and has scored 15 times.
