Arizona, Washington Agree to Swap
Monday, July 9th , 2035
It came down to the last hour of a self-imposed trade deadline, but the Arizona Diamondbacks and Washington Nationals finally came to an agreement on a trade that had been rumored for weeks. The trade sends Arizona's 23-year old minor league CF Jimmy Cronin and 22-year old minor league LF Raheem Sephus to Washington in a swap for 22-year old minor league SS Andy Donaldson, 23-year old minor league RHP Jimmy Clark, and 23-year old minor league 2B Luis Ayala. Both GMs seemed relieved that the deal had been completed with one stating, "We had to set a deadline to get it done." The players are expected to report to their new teams by tomorrow.
