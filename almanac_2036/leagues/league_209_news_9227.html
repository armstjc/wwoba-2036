Rancho Cucamonga Loses Ernie McLaughlin, Will Miss Season
Friday, June 1st , 2035
Ernie McLaughlin is doubtful to make the playoffs this year, according to sources within the Rancho Cucamonga organization. Although the injury was initially thought to be not serious, tests have revealed the player will be out up to 6 months with a torn labrum. The 22-year-old starting pitcher suffered the injury throwing a pitch in a recent game against the Inland Empire 66ers.

McLaughlin has made 10 starts so far this year and put up a 4-4 won-lost record with a 4.57 ERA. In 61 innings he has allowed 57 hits, walked 31 and struck out 56.
