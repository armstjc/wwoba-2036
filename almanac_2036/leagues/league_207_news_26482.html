Hickory Hurler Tops in Outstanding Pitcher Award Voting
Saturday, October 18th , 2036
Alejandro Marulanda of the Hickory Crawdads will be adding a new centerpiece to his mantel after being tabbed for the South Atlantic League Outstanding Pitcher Award today.

The 24-year-old hurler started 25 games this season, tallying a 14-9 record. In 183.1 innings, Marulanda gave up 141 hits, struck out 181, walked 65 and held opponents to a .214 average as he posted a fine 2.95 ERA.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 12. Mike Eggleton of the Jersey Shore BlueClaws finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Tyshawn Ambroise of the Bowling Green Hot Rods finished third.
