Dayton-West Michigan Fight Results in Suspensions
Saturday, May 26th , 2035
Sam Louvar of the Dayton Dragons and Ilari Fusarpoli of the West Michigan Whitecaps will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 5 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Dragons-Whitecaps game at Day Air Ballpark.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Louvar and Fusarpoli will begin serving them soon.

So far on the season, Louvar is hitting .400 with 1 home run, 2 RBIs and 4 runs scored. He has 6 hits and played in 8 games.

This season in 7 starts, Ilari Fusarpoli has a record of 0-4 with 25 strikeouts in 29.2 innings and a 6.67 ERA.
