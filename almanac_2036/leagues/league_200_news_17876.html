MLB Headline News: First-Year Player Draft
Tuesday, May 6th , 2036
Lost in all the hoopla of today's amateur draft are the young men who won't be going first, tenth or even fiftieth. Many of them will probably never make Major League Baseball rosters, but one and all will be spurred on by the possibility that someday they'll be playing on a championship club. San Francisco, Detroit, Washington, Seattle and Minnesota will have the first five choices. BNN analysts report that these players are likely to be high picks in the 2036 draft:


1. Greg Deats, 22 year-old 2B out of college.
2. Pat Mock, 17 year-old SS out of high-school.
3. David Montalvo, 18 year-old CF out of high-school.
4. Eric Berndt, 19 year-old RHP out of high-school.
5. Tommy Perry, 18 year-old RHP out of high-school.
6. Chris Vineyard, 18 year-old 2B out of high-school.
7. Piersen Helms, 17 year-old SS out of high-school.
8. Alejandro Castro, 18 year-old 3B out of high-school.
9. Yoshiaki Nakamura, 18 year-old CF out of high-school.
10. Julio Mendoza, 18 year-old 1B out of high-school.
