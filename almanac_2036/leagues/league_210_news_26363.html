Best CAR Reliever Named
Wednesday, October 15th , 2036
Jim Vannucci was a calming influence out of the Down East Wood Ducks bullpen this season.

His teammates and manager said he had a poised demeanor about him that was typical of someone much older and more experienced. His poise has paid off, as the 24-year-old turned in a sparkling season and has been named the winner of the Carolina League Reliever of the Year for 2036.

The Ojai, California native won the award with 24 saves in 48 relief appearances, 80 strikeouts in 58.1 innings and a record of 5-3 to go with his 1.08 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 12. Jon Bassing of the Columbia Fireflies finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Joe Weaver of the Fredericksburg Nationals finished third.
