Kwon Wins Best Bat Award in September
Monday, October 1st , 2035
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 25 Sa-yoon Kwon has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Samsung Lions star can enjoy the numbers he put up in September, which have garnered him the nod as the Korean Baseball Organization Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .348 average with 6 home runs, 22 RBIs and 21 runs scored.

This season, Kwon has hit 21 home runs and posted a .316 batting average.
