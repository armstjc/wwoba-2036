Ornelas Gets 2-Game Suspension
Friday, June 6th , 2036
The Montgomery Biscuits were not very happy with the home plate umpire in today's loss to Mississippi at Montgomery Riverwalk Stadium. One in particular -- Lazaro Ornelas. Upon being called out on strikes, the Biscuits shortstop gave the ump quite an earful. For that, the man in blue gave him the thumb and sent him to an early shower.

But the disgruntled Ornelas lingered on the field for another five minutes, berating the umpire. Finally his manager got him off the field and into the clubhouse.

But it got worse after the game, when Ornelas told reporters, "He was awful out there. They played 'Three Blind Mice' for him."

For his escapades and after-the-game oration, the league office suspended Ornelas for 2 games.

Thus far this season, Ornelas is hitting .236 with 6 home runs, 20 RBIs and 24 runs scored. He has 34 hits in 144 at-bats and has played in 46 games.
