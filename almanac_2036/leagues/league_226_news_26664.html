#1 Pitcher Trophy Won by Mighty Mussels Escalante
Wednesday, October 22nd , 2036
This year, there was no one better on the mound than Luis Escalante. Posting 16 wins and a 1.42 ERA was only part of the story for the Fort Myers superstar. For his outstanding performance in 2036 he was chosen as the Florida State League Pitcher of the Year Award winner.

From start to finish Escalante never let up. In 27 starts he recorded 16 wins and 1 loss, 171.2 innings, 199 strikeouts, 22 walks, yielded 122 hits, and permitted opposing batters to hit .197 against him.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Esteban Reyna of the Tampa Tarpons finished second in voting, while Mike Schoenfeld of the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels finished third.
