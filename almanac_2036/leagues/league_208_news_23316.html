Players Suspended in River Bandits-Chiefs Donnybrook
Friday, August 8th , 2036
Yesterday at Modern Woodmen Park Rogelio Guerrero and Andres Villalobos got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Quad Cities River Bandits and Peoria Chiefs. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.
