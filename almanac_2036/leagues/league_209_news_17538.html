Storm Parrish to Miss Year
Tuesday, April 29th , 2036
When Andy Parrish was hurt against the Rancho Cucamonga Quakes, the Lake Elsinore Storm were hopeful that the injury was not serious. However, it proved to be more severe than first thought. The 21-year-old starting pitcher suffered a torn rotator cuff throwing a pitch in yesterday's game. Team doctors told BNN today that Parrish would miss 12 months of the season.
