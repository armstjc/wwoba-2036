Aberdeen Beats Bowling Green in Round 2
Saturday, October 4th , 2036
The Aberdeen Ironbirds earned their 1st Round 2 title and now have all winter to spend basking in the glory, while the beaten Bowling Green Hot Rods will have plenty of time to wonder what went wrong.

Aberdeen became the South Atlantic League champion for 2036 with a 3-2 triumph to capture the series 4-1.

"Both clubs are talented. We just made fewer mistakes than they did," winning manager Miguel Jimenez said to the press corps at the postgame press conference. "We made the crucial plays and got the timely hits."

Bowling Green skipper Giuseppe Chiaramonte agreed with that assessment, saying his club turned out to be "a little weak on fundamentals" at crucial times.

"I have a sneaking suspicion we will be working on fundamentals a lot next spring," he said.

On the way to the Round 2 championship, the Ironbirds ended up in second place in the Northern Division and compiled a 88-44 record during the regular season.
