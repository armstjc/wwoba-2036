League Issues Lim 4-Game Suspension
Thursday, August 9th , 2035
The LG Twins were not very happy with the home plate umpire in today's loss to Lotte at Sajik Baseball Stadium. One in particular -- Seo-joon Lim. Upon being called out on strikes, the Twins third baseman gave the ump quite an earful. For that, the man in blue gave him the thumb and sent him to an early shower.

But the disgruntled Lim lingered on the field for another five minutes, berating the umpire. Finally his manager got him off the field and into the clubhouse.

But it got worse after the game, when Lim told reporters, "He was awful out there. They played 'Three Blind Mice' for him."

For his escapades and after-the-game oration, the league office suspended Lim for 4 games.

This year, Lim has collected a .248 batting average to date with 77 hits in 311 at-bats, 25 RBIs, 5 home runs and scored 27 times. He has played in 93 games.
