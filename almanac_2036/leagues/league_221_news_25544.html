DSL Outstanding Pitcher Award Winner Announced
Thursday, September 25th , 2036
Texas (DSL) ace Willie Petty put up some sensational stats for the 2036 season and has been voted the Dominican Rookie League Outstanding Pitcher Award winner.

The 23-year-old Rangers star made 13 starts and racked up a 11-1 record. He pitched 108.1 innings, collected 123 strikeouts, gave up 10 walks and 82 hits in holding the opposition to a .204 batting average.
