Great Week for Sangdong's Sang-hyuk Kim
Monday, August 25th , 2036
Sang-hyuk Kim must have thought he was dreaming. First he went on an exceptional week-long batting spree, bashing the ball at a .545 clip, then the Sangdong third baseman finds out he has been chosen the league's best performer and gets the KFL Player of the Week Award.

He told BNN reporters in the postgame, "It was a great week for me. I could see the ball so well. Plus some of the balls seemed to have good eyes, too."

For the week Kim batted .545 (12-for-22) with 3 home runs and 9 RBIs.

In the current season, Kim has put up a .384 batting average with 33 hits, 3 home runs, 16 RBI and scored 19 runs.
