CAR Reliever of the Year Winner Named
Monday, October 15th , 2035
At the young age of only 23, Jim Vannucci has been named the Carolina League Reliever of the Year winner for 2035.

The future appears bright for the Down East Wood Ducks relief ace, who captured the award with 33 saves in 50 relief appearances, 66 strikeouts in 58 innings and a record of 5-1 to go with his 1.24 ERA.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Ernesto Cienfuegos of the Salem Red Sox finished second in voting, while Tim Villicana of the Myrtle Beach Pelicans finished third.
