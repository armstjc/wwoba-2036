Billy Meyers Sidelined for Season
Tuesday, September 11th , 2035
Doctors in Dayton said Billy Meyers might not play anymore this year. The Dragons starting pitcher suffered bone chips in his elbow during a game against the Wisconsin Timber Rattlers and will be out for at least 5 months. Club officials told BNN they do not expect him to return to the team this season.

So far this season, Meyers has 8 wins and 9 losses and a 4.09 ERA.
