KBO Gives #1 Pitcher Award
Thursday, November 8th , 2035
Eagles ace Mun-yeong Kim is the latest Korean Baseball Organization Outstanding Pitcher Award trophy winner. The Hanwha star shined on the diamond time and again this year and finished with an outstanding 12-7 record.

In 31 starts he tossed 191.2 innings, posted 262 strikeouts and sported a 2.77 ERA. Opposing batters hit .171 when Kim was pitching.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Won-keun Im of the kt wiz finished second in voting, while Jung-sang Kim of the LG Twins finished third.
