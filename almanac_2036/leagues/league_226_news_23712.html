Udeh Belts 3 HR Against Dunedin
Friday, August 15th , 2036
Davon Udeh went on an explosive hitting spree against the Dunedin Blue Jays at TD Ballpark, launching a trio of blasts to help the Lakeland Flying Tigers win, 13-4.

The first baseman finished 4-for-5 with 3 runs scored and 6 runs batted in.

"It was just one of those days," Udeh said in a postgame interview. "I don't know how to explain it. I'm just putting a good swing on the ball."

Dunedin skipper Dallas McPherson said Udeh was "locked-in."

"We made some pretty good pitches," he said. "Those first two homers were exactly where the pitcher wanted to put them. He's just hot right now -- you've just got to tip your hat to him."
