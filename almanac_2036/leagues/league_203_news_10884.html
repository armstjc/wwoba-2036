Mohawk Is June's Top Starter
Sunday, July 1st , 2035
Capping off a whirlwind month in which he went 5-0 with a 1.45 ERA, Bob Mohawk was selected as June's Eastern League Pitcher of the Month today. Reporters caught up with the elated Harrisburg starter as he left the clubhouse.

"I know it's difficult for pitchers -- when you have a bad start or two, people start wondering if you've lost it. Then you ride stretches like this one for all you can. You find it easier to come to the park every day because know you're contributing to the club, not just waiting for your turn in the rotation to make up for your last start."

Bob Mohawk definitely contributed to the club. He had 5 starts, throwing 37.1 innings, striking out 18, and holding the opposition to a .169 average.

For the season, Mohawk has a record of 7 wins and 2 losses in 9 appearances. He has an ERA of 2.91 and has fanned 36 batters in 65 innings.
