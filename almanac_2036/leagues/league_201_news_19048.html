Johnson Boosts Hit Streak to 20 Games
Friday, May 23rd , 2036
Dave Johnson, catcher for the Toledo Mud Hens, ran his consecutive-game hitting streak to 20 games with 3 hits in his club's 6-2 victory over the Louisville Bats at Louisville Slugger Field.

"Not the outcome we wanted," Louisville manager Greg Brown said. "There's never a good time to lose, and it's never fun to be on the wrong end of somebody else's accomplishment."
Dave Johnson hit an RBI single in the 1st, struck out in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Alfredo Ferreira in the 6th, singled in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.

So far this year, Johnson has 7 home runs and a .344 batting average.
