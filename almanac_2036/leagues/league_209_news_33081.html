Benton of Modesto Named Best Pitcher
Tuesday, October 16th , 2035
A bullpen specialist has captured the California League Outstanding Pitcher Award for 2035. The top vote-getter was Sambu Benton of the Modesto Nuts, who finished the year with 7 saves in 7 save opportunities.

He had 9 wins and 2 losses with a 1.82 ERA. Benton hurled 123.1 innings and totaled 122 strikeouts.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Brad Brown of the Fresno Grizzlies finished second in voting, while Russell Caffyn of the Lake Elsinore Storm finished third.
