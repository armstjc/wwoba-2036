Kansas City Captures American League Central Division Crown
Friday, September 14th , 2035
A season's worth of hard work has culminated in a division championship for the Kansas City Royals.

The club officially clinched its spot atop the American League Central Division today, with plenty of games to spare.

"It feels good to get it wrapped up early and now be able to set ourselves up for the playoffs," said reliever Ben Bess.

The club currently has a 89-57 record and this is their 11th division championship.

"We want to bring the 3rd World Series to Kansas City," said Moises Zorrilla. "We think we've got what it takes to be the best in Major League Baseball."
