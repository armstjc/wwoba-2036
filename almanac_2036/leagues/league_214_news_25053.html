Best ACL Reliever Named
Sunday, September 14th , 2036
Kymani Hockless was a calming influence out of the Chicago A (ACL) White Sox bullpen this season.

His teammates and manager said he had a poised demeanor about him that was typical of someone much older and more experienced. His poise has paid off, as the 19-year-old turned in a sparkling season and has been named the winner of the Arizona Complex League Reliever of the Year for 2036.

The Loveland, Colorado native won the award with 1 save in 28 relief appearances, 49 strikeouts in 48.1 innings and a record of 3-1 to go with his 1.86 ERA.

He received 15 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Gahlen Bouton of the Texas (ACL) Rangers finished second in voting, while Johnny Adams of the Kansas City (ACL) Royals finished third.
