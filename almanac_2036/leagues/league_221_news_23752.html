Milwaukee (DSL) Brewers Win Top Spot in San Pedro de Macoris Division
Sunday, August 17th , 2036
The Milwaukee (DSL) Brewers are champions of the San Pedro de Macoris Division, but don't plan the parade quite yet.

"It's a nice accomplishment, but we haven't done anything until we win the Round 3," said reliever Ezequiel Cartagena. "We're going to celebrate this for sure, but the real celebration is still to come for fans in Milwaukee (DSL)."

The club's record currently sits at 41-23. It is the team's 6th division title and they will be seeking the franchise's 2nd Dominican Rookie League championship.

Team manager Noah Murdock said he plans to take some time to celebrate the division title, but then it's "back to work" for members of the team and coaching staff.
