Week's Best Player: Daytona's Corredor
Monday, August 6th , 2035
Daytona fans have something to crow about today as Daytona catcher Vito Corredor nabbed the Florida State League Player of the Week Award for his performance over the last seven days.

"I know there are times when it's hard to lay off pitches. Everyone goes through stretches when he's too eager to put the ball in play. You know, though, I feel like this past week I was able to get in the right frame of mind and just let things come naturally," said Corredor.

He posted a .360 average (9-for-25) this past week with 3 home runs, 8 RBIs, and 7 runs scored.

Looking at his present stats, Corredor has compiled a .276 batting average in 79 games with a .385 on-base percentage, while hitting 16 home runs and driving in 49 runs.
