Los Angeles-Seattle Deal Confirmed
Friday, March 30th , 2035
Los Angeles and Seattle announced today in a joint press conference that 23-year old minor league CF Mellal Saidi-Sief, 20-year old minor league LF Juan Lozano, and 21-year old minor league LF Juan Balandran will be going to the Mariners in exchange for 36-year old RHP Taylor Dollard. "It'll be exciting to join a new organization," one player said. "Some of us could use a fresh start." The players have each passed physicals and are expected to report by day's end.

Seattle will retain a portion of Dollard's remaining contract.

Dollard has a career won-loss record of 142 wins and 79 losses with 1826 strikeouts in 1890.1 innings and a 3.13 ERA.
