Agosto Rewarded with 2035 SAL Most Valuable Player Award
Tuesday, October 16th , 2035
At the age of 22, Nesty Agosto of the Aberdeen Ironbirds could very well have his best years ahead of him.

It's a startling thought when you consider the year he had in 2035 was good enough to win the second baseman the South Atlantic League Most Valuable Player Award.

"See the ball, hit the ball," he said. "That's my basic approach."

The top hitter put up a .292 batting average this year, as well as a .387 on-base percentage. He played 98 games and racked up 113 hits, 32 doubles, 5 triples, 21 home runs, 78 RBIs and 75 runs scored.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 12. Mike Mack of the Hudson Valley Renegades finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Josh Moore of the Aberdeen Ironbirds finished third.
