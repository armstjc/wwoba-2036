South Atlantic League Picks Skipper of the Year
Friday, October 17th , 2036
Bowling Green Hot Rods field manager Giuseppe Chiaramonte has brought a winning attitude to the team and the city. His no-nonsense approach, work ethic and baseball experience have paved the way for success.

This season the Hot Rods parlayed his managerial skills and some talented ballplayers into a first place spot in the Southern Division standings. Bowling Green finished the year with a 94-38 record.

To everybody's delight Chiaramonte was voted the 2036 South Atlantic League Manager of the Year by the baseball writers.

"Mr. Chiaramonte doesn't take any nonsense. If you aren't doing your job, he's not afraid to call you on it," noted one of his ballplayers. "But the team wouldn't want to play for anyone else. He's tough, demanding, but he treats everyone fairly and doesn't play favorites. Nobody gets pampered on his ballclub."
