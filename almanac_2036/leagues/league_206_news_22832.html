NWL's Best Batter Announced
Friday, August 1st , 2036
Even though he is a newcomer to Northwest League, Joey Fritz put up some good numbers in July and earned the NWL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Eugene's offensive leaders with a .275 average in 24 games. He also racked up 9 home runs, 20 RBIs, and 14 runs scored.

This year, Fritz has hit at a .308 clip with 109 hits, 34 home runs, 82 RBIs and 55 runs scored.
