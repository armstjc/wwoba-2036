Ignacio Nieto Injured, Out for Season
Sunday, August 31st , 2036
The doctors have spoken and Ignacio Nieto of the Corpus Christi Hooks didn't like what he heard. The 22-year-old reliever is almost certainly gone for the rest of the year and depending on his progress could miss some of next season as well. Nieto sustained his season-ending injury during the Amarillo Sod Poodles game yesterday at Hodgetown. He had to leave the game because he hurt himself while throwing a pitch. Today the Corpus Christi medical staff reported Nieto had a damaged elbow ligament and would be absent from the lineup for at least 10-11 months.

For the season, Nieto has made 7 relief appearances, recorded no saves and compiled a 1-0 won-lost mark and a 2.45 ERA. In pitching 7.1 innings, he has totaled 2 strikeouts and no walks.
