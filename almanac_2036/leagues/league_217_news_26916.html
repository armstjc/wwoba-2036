KBO's #1 Player for 2036 Named
Thursday, October 30th , 2036
When it came to hitting the baseball, Sa-yoon Kwon had few peers in the Korean Baseball Organization this year. The gifted second baseman for Samsung tagged opposing pitchers for a .315 average and was voted the Most Valuable Player Award for 2036. Included in his season stats were 128 hits, 20 doubles, 3 triples, 25 home runs, 69 runs scored and 72 RBIs.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 10. Hye-min Kim of the Hanwha Eagles finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Gye-hyun Jeon of the kt wiz finished third.
