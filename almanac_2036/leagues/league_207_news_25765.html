Dowden Grabs SAL Best Batter Award for September
Wednesday, October 1st , 2036
Chris Dowden made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the South Atlantic League this past month.

The 23-year-old hit .327 with 6 home runs, 12 RBIs and 16 runs scored.
