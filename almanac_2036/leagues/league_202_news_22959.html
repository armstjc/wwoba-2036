Holmes Scorches Dodgers for 3 HR
Sunday, August 3rd , 2036
Whatever Ben Holmes ate for breakfast before his game against the Oklahoma City Dodgers, it gave him some impressive power.

The Tacoma first baseman smacked three balls up and out of the park at Cheney Stadium. His home run barrage helped the Rainiers defeat the Dodgers, 6-1.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 3-for-4, scored 3 times, drove in 4 and put on a display that history will remember.

Ben Holmes hit a solo-shot off Jaime Swart in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Jaime Swart in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Ron Gay in the 8th.

In 46 games Holmes is hitting .300 this season with 48 hits, 18 home runs, 43 RBIs, 22 walks, .397 OBP and has scored 35 runs.
