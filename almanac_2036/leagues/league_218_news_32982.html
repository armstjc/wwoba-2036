2035 Outstanding Pitcher Award Winner Announced
Saturday, October 13th , 2035
Icheon ace Dong-su Kim put up some sensational stats for the 2035 season and has been voted the Korean Futures League Outstanding Pitcher Award winner.

The 24-year-old Twins star made 23 starts and racked up a 8-7 record. He pitched 156 innings, collected 140 strikeouts, gave up 81 walks and 180 hits in holding the opposition to a .290 batting average.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Hae-chan Kim of the Gyeongsan Lions finished second in voting, while Dae-ho Kim of the Icheon Bears finished third.
