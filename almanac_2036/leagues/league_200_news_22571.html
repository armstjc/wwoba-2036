Toronto Trades McKee for Los Angeles's Peralta
Monday, July 28th , 2036
It took some time to iron out the details, but the Blue Jays and Angels have agreed to a swap. Los Angeles will get 17-year old minor league RHP Andy Bionde and 22-year old minor league 1B Jonathan McKee with Toronto receiving 27-year old 3B Juan Peralta in return. A source close to the trade talks said the two clubs were initially looking to make a bigger deal, but discussions broke off late last night. The two sides reconvened early today and eventually settled on this deal.

Los Angeles will retain a portion of Peralta's remaining contract.

This season, Peralta is swinging the bat at a .248 rate with 65 hits in 262 at-bats. To date he has hit 21 home runs, scored 37 runs and driven in 64.
