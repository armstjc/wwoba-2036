Atlanta (DSL)-Milwaukee (DSL) Brawl, Essien and Barcena Suspended
Saturday, June 16th , 2035
Omari Essien of the Atlanta (DSL) Braves and Juan Barcena of the Milwaukee (DSL) Brewers will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Braves-Brewers game at Tower Complex.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Essien and Barcena will begin serving them soon.
