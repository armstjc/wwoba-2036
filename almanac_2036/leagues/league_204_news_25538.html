Birmingham's Clee Down with Injury
Wednesday, September 24th , 2036
Doctors in Birmingham said Joe Clee might not play anymore this year. The Barons catcher suffered a damaged elbow ligament during a game against the Tennessee Smokies and will be out for at least 12-13 months. Club officials told BNN they do not expect him to return to the team this season.

Clee is swinging at a .234 pace on the year with 11 home runs, 46 RBIs and 48 runs scored. He has played in 107 games.
