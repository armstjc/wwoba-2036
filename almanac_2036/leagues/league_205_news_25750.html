Midland Wins Round 1
Tuesday, September 30th , 2036
It's a sweet sweep for the Midland RockHounds and they have won the Round 1. The Frisco RoughRiders were not able to keep pace with the RockHounds and dropped the series in short order, losing, 4-3, in the deciding game.

"This wasn't really the effort we hoped for," said Frisco manager Rob Burger.

It's now on to the Round 2 for Midland after their 3-0 series win.

Leading the way for the winners was second baseman Ted Kimball, who was named MVP. For the series, Kimball hit .400 with a .438 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Midland RockHounds don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Arkansas Travelers - Tulsa Drillers series. Arkansas leads the series 2 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
