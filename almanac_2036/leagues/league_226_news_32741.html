The Tampa faithful were cheering in the streets, and Clearwater fans wiping away the tears, after the Tarpons won the Round 2 at George M. Steinbrenner Field today.

The Tarpons clinched it by knocking off the Threshers 12-5 to take their 3rd Florida State League crown. Tampa took the series 4-1.

"This club never gave up all year long," Tampa manager Jonathan Mota told the press. "They believed they could win the title and they did."

On the way to the Round 2 title, Tampa compiled a 72-60 won-loss record and finished third in the West Division.

"We just didn't play well enough to win," Threshers manager Luke Carlin said. "We just didn't make the big plays like they did."
