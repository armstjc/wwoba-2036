Kwon Slams 3 Deep for Samsung
Tuesday, September 4th , 2035
Sa-yoon Kwon was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 10-4 Samsung Lions triumph at Daegu Samsung Lions Park.

The 25-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Lions past the SSG Landers.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Kwon told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The second baseman went 5-for-5, rung up 8 RBIs and scored 3 runs on his record-setting day.

Sa-yoon Kwon hit a three-run home run off Myeong-seong Jeon in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Joon-young Kim in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off Joon-young Kim in the 7th.

For the year, Kwon has hit at a .318 clip with 76 RBIs, 19 home runs and 78 runs scored.
