Edgar Flores Nabs Best Player Trophy
Wednesday, October 17th , 2035
Edgar Flores took California League by storm this year, and he did it all at the age of 21.

For his spectacular campaign, the Fresno Grizzlies star has been named the 2035 California League Most Valuable Player Award recipient.

"To be considered amongst the best in the league is overwhelming," said the right fielder.

On the season he compiled a .309 batting average to go with a .411 on-base percentage. The native of Samana, Dominican Republic also added 26 doubles, 3 triples, 23 home runs, 79 RBIs and 72 runs scored.

He received 4 first place votes out of a possible 8. Brad Brown of the Fresno Grizzlies finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Edgar Padilla of the Lake Elsinore Storm finished third.
