Tampa's Pigram Wins FSL Most Valuable Player Award
Thursday, October 23rd , 2036
Kymani Pigram of the Tampa Tarpons had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 2036.

The 21-year-old third baseman put up a .307 average and a .391 on-base percentage, piling up 155 hits, 36 doubles, 5 triples, 37 home runs, 119 RBIs and 93 runs scored.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 10. Juan Correa of the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Eric Christy of the Tampa Tarpons finished third with 1 first place vote.
