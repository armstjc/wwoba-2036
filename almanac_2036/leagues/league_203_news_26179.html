Top EL Pitcher Announced
Sunday, October 12th , 2036
Chase Dollander of the Fisher Cats humbled hitters all season long and today celebrated as he hauled in the Eastern League Pitcher of the Year trophy, the coveted Outstanding Pitcher Award.

The New Hampshire hurler held opposing hitters to a .251 batting average while putting up a 12-6 record and a 3.30 earned run average. He started 25 games, pitched 180 innings and struck out 123.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 12. Byron Dogherty of the Somerset Patriots finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Dave Bowles of the Altoona Curve finished third with 1 first place vote.
