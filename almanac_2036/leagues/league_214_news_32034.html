Guerrero Grabs ACL Reliever of the Year
Tuesday, September 18th , 2035
He may only be 22, but Yoeilin Guerrero of the Seattle (ACL) Mariners has already established himself as one of the elite relievers in the Arizona Complex League.

The reliever was today named the ACL Reliever of the Year winner for 2035.

He won the award with a strong year that saw him put up 18 saves in 27 relief appearances and 40 strikeouts in 34 innings. He had a record of 2-2 to go with his 1.32 ERA.

He received 15 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Jon Ayers of the Colorado (ACL) Rockies finished second in voting, while Mike Pentek of the Milwaukee (ACL) Brewers Gold finished third.
