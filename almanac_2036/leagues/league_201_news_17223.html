Doyle Goes on Rampage Against Jumbo Shrimp
Wednesday, April 23rd , 2036
Jamie Doyle was at the head of a Nashville hit parade at First Horizon Park. The center fielder went to the plate 5 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the Sounds to a nice 12-2 victory over the Jacksonville Jumbo Shrimp.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Doyle told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

Jumbo Shrimp skipper Mike Johns said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Jamie Doyle singled in the 2nd, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 8th.

Hitting .242 to date, Doyle has 1 home run and 6 RBIs. He has played in 16 games.
