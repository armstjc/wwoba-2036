Yoo Named June's #1 Newcomer
Sunday, July 1st , 2035
The Rookie of the Month Award has been announced and the Korean Baseball Organization winner is SSG hurler, Hyun-woo Yoo. For his fine efforts in June he has been named the league's #1 rookie.

During the month in 5 starts, Yoo was a standout performer with a 1.04 ERA and 4 wins and no losses. In 26 innings he struck out 28 and gave up 21 hits and 14 walks.

On his stats sheet for the year Yoo shows a 9-2 record, 14 starts, 70 innings, 68 strikeouts and a 3.73 ERA.
