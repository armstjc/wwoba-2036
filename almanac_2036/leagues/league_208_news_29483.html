Captains McCovery Named MWL's Best Pitcher
Wednesday, August 1st , 2035
The hottest hurler in the Midwest League last month was Sean McCovery of the Lake County Captains. He caught the attention of everybody in the league with some eye-catching stats, good enough to win the MWL Pitcher of the Month.

In July, McCovery starred with an impressive 4-2 record. In 6 starts he gave up 30 base hits and struck out 48 in 43.1 innings with a 1.66 ERA.

So far this year, McCovery has made 20 starts, tossed 133.2 innings, struck out 130 batters and walked 58. His record is 10-5 with a 3.03 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .237 batting average.
