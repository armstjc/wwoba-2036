Palm Beach-Dunedin Brawl, Mills and Alvarado Suspended
Saturday, September 13th , 2036
Jim Mills of the Palm Beach Cardinals and Alfredo Alvarado of the Dunedin Blue Jays will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 8 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Cardinals-Blue Jays game at TD Ballpark.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Mills and Alvarado will begin serving them soon.

So far in 2036 Mills is batting .197 with 4 home runs, 36 RBIs and 46 runs scored. He has 91 hits in 461 at-bats and a .294 OBP.

This year, Alvarado has a 5-8 record and a 3.43 ERA.
