Salguero Wins CAL Player of the Week Honors
Monday, June 30th , 2036
A red-hot bat made Daniel Salguero the obvious choice for this week's California League Player of the Week.

The Visalia second baseman laid down some numbers that no one else could match, including a .571 average on 8 hits in 14 at-bats, 6 home runs, 13 RBIs, and 7 runs scored. Say no more ... he got the award.

For the year, Salguero has hit at a .282 clip with 44 RBIs, 13 home runs and 38 runs scored.
