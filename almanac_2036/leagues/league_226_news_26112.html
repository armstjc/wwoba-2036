Round 2 to Threshers
Wednesday, October 8th , 2036
It was a hard-fought battle for both teams to make it this far. But as the saying goes, "to the victors go the spoils." For the victorious Clearwater Threshers, that meant the Florida State League Round 2 trophy.

The Threshers took a 4-3 victory over the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels at Hammond Stadium, winning their 5th championship after taking the series 4-3.

"We've got grit," said Threshers third baseman Nelson Maya, who was named MVP of the series. "We know how to win when we have to."

The new titleholders completed the regular season with a second-place finish in the West Division and a 92-41 record.
