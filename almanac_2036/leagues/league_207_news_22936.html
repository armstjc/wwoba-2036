Lamboy Stops Hickory Cold, Renegades Win, 4-0
Saturday, August 2nd , 2036
The Hudson Valley Renegades shut out the Hickory Crawdads, 4-0, behind the strong pitching of Mauricio Lamboy. Lamboy surrendered only 5 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 8.

Following the last out, Lamboy appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Hickory manager Kelvin Nova, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken left-hander as the clubs left the field. Lamboy's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Crawdads skipper would not comment on the exchange but, at the Renegades press conference, Lamboy confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Lamboy said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Hudson Valley manager William Penniall added.
