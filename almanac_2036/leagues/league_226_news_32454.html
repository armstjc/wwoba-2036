Clearwater Takes Round 1
Wednesday, September 26th , 2035
The Clearwater Threshers have swept the Daytona Tortugas and are on to the Round 2. Clearwater won 9-1 today at BayCare Ballpark to clinch the Round 1.

"We're happy to have put the dagger in when we had the chance," said Clearwater center fielder Chance Bloom, who was named MVP for the series. "It could have been a tough series to finish if we let them start winning."

Bloom won the award with a .556 batting average and a .550 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 8 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Clearwater Threshers don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Tampa Tarpons - Fort Myers Mighty Mussels series. Tampa leads the series 2 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
