Nationals Hitless Against Rodgers
Wednesday, June 4th , 2036
The Washington Nationals had no answer for pitcher Eric Rodgers and the St. Louis Cardinals. Rodgers tossed a no-hitter against the Nationals, totaling 9 strikeouts and 1 walk in an impressive 1-0 win at Busch Stadium.

"Everything was working today," he told reporters following the game. "I had excellent location and great movement on my pitches. Good things happen when all of it comes together like that."

The Washington clubhouse emptied out quickly following the game. Nationals skipper Joel Booker joked that it was "as silent as our bats were today."

"I don't have much to say to you guys right now," he told BNN reporters. "I'm speechless after that performance."

This year, Rodgers has a 4-4 record and a 2.69 ERA.
