LG Loses Ji-yong Lee, Will Miss Season
Wednesday, July 4th , 2035
The doctors have spoken and Ji-yong Lee of the LG Twins didn't like what he heard. The 35-year-old starting pitcher is almost certainly gone for the rest of the year and depending on his progress could miss some of next season as well. Lee sustained his season-ending injury during the NC Dinos game yesterday at Jamsil Baseball Stadium(LG). He had to leave the game because he hurt himself while throwing a pitch. Today the LG medical staff reported Lee had shoulder inflammation and would be absent from the lineup for at least 4-5 months.

Lee has posted a 3-4 record for the year with a 6.06 ERA. Teams are hitting .308 against him and he has rung up 58 strikeouts in 62.1 innings.
