Rainer on Fire: 5-for-6 for KIA
Sunday, July 13th , 2036
Even as a kid, Bryce Rainer said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Suwon KT Wiz Park, Rainer showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 31-year-old third baseman had 5 hits in 6 at-bats for the KIA Tigers, helping his team knock off the kt wiz by a score of 11-2.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did."

Bryce Rainer doubled in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Bit-garam Kim in the 2nd, doubled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, singled in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.

Rainer is currently hitting at a .261 pace with 7 home runs, 28 RBIs and has scored 45 times.
