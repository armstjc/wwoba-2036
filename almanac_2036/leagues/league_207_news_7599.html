Picoteiro Is King of Swing: Goes Deep 3 Times
Wednesday, May 2nd , 2035
Cristovão Picoteiro went yard three times in a Hudson Valley Renegades victory over the Wilmington Blue Rocks at Dutchess Stadium.

"I was in a groove," he said. "Every pitch they tossed up there looked like a grapefruit."

For the day, the Hudson Valley center fielder went 4-for-5 against Wilmington with 7 RBIs and 3 runs scored in a brilliant display of power hitting that led the Renegades to the 16-5 win.

"I tell you what. I'm shocked because I didn't think that last ball had enough to get out," he told BNN reporters in the clubhouse following the game. "I thought it was just going to be a long out."

Cristovão Picoteiro flied out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Shamel Menifee in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off T.J. LaNoue in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off T.J. LaNoue in the 8th.

This season, Picoteiro has hit at a .195 clip and produced 12 RBI, 3 home runs and 6 runs.
