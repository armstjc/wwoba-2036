Korean Baseball Organization Selects Best Rookie
Monday, October 27th , 2036
2036 was a much better year than Myon-ki Kim expected. This spring he was overjoyed at just making the kt roster. Not only did he make the team, he was the starting first baseman. Then it got even better.

In his debut season, Kim hit .249 with 115 hits in 461 at-bats, 18 home runs, 62 RBIs, 54 bases on balls and a .341 on-base percentage -- good enough numbers to be honored with the Korean Baseball Organization Newcomer of the Year Award.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 10. Dong-su Kim of the LG Twins finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Jung-hoon Kim of the KIA Tigers finished third.
