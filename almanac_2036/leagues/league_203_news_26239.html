Monday, October 13th , 2036
The future looks extremely bright for Daniel Aldaco. At the age of 23, many players are still trying to find their way in Eastern League.

Aldaco has not only found his way, he's already established himself as one of the game's brightest stars, today being named the winner of the Eastern League Most Valuable Player Award.

"I didn't expect to hit .319 this year, that's for sure," the center fielder said.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 12. Ezequiel Prado of the Reading Fightin Phils finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Jorge Aguirre of the Akron RubberDucks finished third.
